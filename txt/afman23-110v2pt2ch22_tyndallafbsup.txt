This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply System Equipment Management. It
applies to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve, and Air Force Guard
organizations supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in contract or support
agreement terms or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims. Send recommended changes and questions
about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF IMT 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. This publication requires collecting and maintaining information protected by the
Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and E.O. 9397. System of records notice
F023 AF IL C applies

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Summary of changes include maximum processing times for equipment requests, commander’s
use of Tyndall AFB Form 17 to appoint designated representatives, delegated authority to sign
1ETX documents, classification and obtain ability of backup appliances, and maintaining the
TAFB Form 17.

22.29.1.2.1. The commander will use Tyndall AFB Form 17, Delete/Delegate Property
