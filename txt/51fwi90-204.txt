This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 90-2, Inspector General – The
Inspection System and complements Air Force Instruction (AFI) 90-201, Inspector General
Activities. This instruction establishes procedures for administering the wing self-inspection
program. It applies to all personnel assigned to the 51st Fighter Wing. Refer recommended
changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR)
using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from
the field through Major Command (MAJCOM) publications/forms managers. Ensure that all
records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with
the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61a/afrims/afrims. The use of the name or mark of any specific manufacturer, commercial
product, commodity, or service in this publication does not imply endorsement by the Air Force.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The changes include replacing all references to 51 FW/IGI with 51 FW/XP. New or revised
material is indicated by asterisks (*).
1. General. All 51 FW organizations will conduct semi-annual self-inspections to ensure
compliance with all applicable United States Air Force (USAF) Major Graded Areas and Sub-
areas, USAF/ Pacific Air Force (PACAF) Special Interest Items, PACAF Mission Performance
Checklists (PACAF Directory (PACAFDIR) 90-XXX) and any additional items deemed
essential to mission performance.
2. Responsibilities:

    2.1. 51 FW/XP will ensure correction, or elevate to the 51 FW/CC, all non-compliance items
    identified by subordinate organizations during their self-inspections.
    2.2. 51 FW/XP will:
       2.2.1. Monitor the progress of self-inspections throughout the 51 FW.
       2.2.2. Make changes to the wing self-inspection program as necessary to enhance
       compliance.
    2.3. 51 FW staff agency chiefs will:
       2.3.1. Ensure every subordinate functional area develops a consolidated self-inspection
       checklist, as described in paragraph 3.1. of this instruction, and performs semi-annual
       self-inspections.
       2.3.2. Correct, or elevate to the 51 FW/XP, all non-compliance items identified by
       subordinate functional areas during their self-inspections.
       2.3.3. Assign a self-inspection monitor who will consolidate and report the results of
       each semi-annual self-inspection in accordance with paragraph 3.1. Appointment letters
       will be completed and forwarded to 51 FW/XP along with the semi-annual self-
       inspection report on the dates specified in paragraph 3.2. An example letter can be found
       in attachment 3 or on the 51 FW/XP Sharepoint.
    2.4. 51 FW group commanders will:
       2.4.1. Ensure every subordinate organization performs semi-annual self-inspections.
       2.4.2. Correct, or elevate to the 51 FW/XP, all non-compliance items identified by
       subordinate organizations during their self-inspections.
       2.4.3. Assign a Group Self-Inspection Monitor who will consolidate and report the
       results of each semi-annual self-inspection in accordance with paragraph 3.1.
       Appointment letters will be completed and forwarded to 51 FW/XP along with the semi-
       annual self-inspection report on the dates specified in paragraph 3.2. An example letter
       can be found in attachment 3 or on the 51 FW/XP Sharepoint.
    2.5. 51 FW group staff agency chiefs will:
       2.5.1. Ensure every subordinate functional area develops a consolidated self-inspection
       checklist, as described in paragraph 3.1. of this instruction, and performs semi-annual
       self-inspections.
       2.5.2. Correct, or elevate to the group commander, all non-compliance items identified
       by subordinate functional areas during their self-inspections.
    2.6. 51 FW squadron commanders will:
       2.6.1. Ensure every subordinate functional area develops a consolidated self-inspection
       checklist, as described in paragraph 3.1. of this instruction, and performs semi-annual
       self-inspections.
       2.6.2. Correct, or elevate to the group commander, all non-compliance items identified
       by subordinate functional areas during their self-inspections.
    2.7. 51 FW functional areas will:

       2.7.1. Develop a consolidated self-inspection checklist as described in paragraph 3.1. of
       this instruction.
       2.7.2. Review and correct self-inspection checklists prior to every semi-annual self-
       inspection.
       2.7.3. Perform semi-annual self-inspections.

       2.7.6. Retain copies of the last two semi-annual self-inspections.
3. Procedures:
   3.1. Functional area self-inspection checklists will be a consolidated list of yes or no
   questions for all applicable compliance items. The consolidated list will be developed from
   the following:      applicable USAF Major Graded Areas and Sub-areas, applicable
   USAF/PACAF Special Interest Items, applicable PACAF Mission Performance Checklists
   (PACAFDIR 90-XXX), local directives and any additional items deemed essential to mission
   performance. Every attempt will be made to keep functional area self-inspection checklists
   limited to only applicable compliance items.
       3.1.1. All current USAF Major Graded Areas and Sub-areas can be found in AFI 90-201.
       3.1.2. All current USAF/PACAF Special Interest Items can be found on the PACAF
       Sharepoint.
       3.1.3. All current PACAF Mission Performance Checklist (PACAFDIR 90-XXX) items
       can be found on the Air Force e-Publishing website. Squadron and staff agency
       functional areas need to analyze all PACAF Mission Performance Checklists
       (PACAFDIR 90-XXX) for possible cross compliance.
   3.2. Semi-Annual self-inspections will occur in every functional area during the months of
   December and June. Semi-annual self-inspection reports will be completed and a semi-
   annual self-inspection report will be forwarded to 51 FW/XP no later than the fifteenth day
   of January and July (see below).
