This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 65-1, Management of Financial Services.
It establishes a reasonable commuting distance as required by the Joint Federal Travel Regulation (JFTR).
This instruction is applicable to all personnel assigned or attached to the 911 Airlift Wing. Refer recom-
mended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using
the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication, as prescribed by AFI 11-215, USAF
Flight Manual Program (FMP). Route AF Form 847 to the 911 AW/FM at Pittsburg IAP-ARS, PA.
Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accor-
dance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://afrims.amc.af.mil/.
This publication is subject to Privacy Act of 1974.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
Due to the closure of the Morgantown Detachment, Attachment 2 and all references to the Morgantown
Detachment were removed.


1. Responsibilities. The unit commanders are responsible to ensure that all reserve orders, AF IMT 938,
Request and Authorization for Active Duty Training/Active Duty Tour or computer generated orders,
are accurately annotated indicating whether the reservist is in a “will commute” or “will not commute”
status during a tour of active duty.

2. Procedures. Attachment 1 is a listing of all areas classified as being within a reasonable commuting
distance of the 911 Airlift Wing. The reasonable commuting distance is listed by city and zip code. If the
city is not listed but the zip code is listed or vice versa, the area is classified as being within the reasonable
commuting distance. A reasonable commute is considered one hour; if you believe your commute is not

reasonable you may file an appeal through your chain of command to the Wing Commander. Include in
your justification a copy of MapQuest from your residence to the 911 AW.

3. Corporate Limits. The city of Coraopolis, PA 15108 is within the corporate limits of the 911 AW and
orders should reflect this information.
