AFI 34-219, Alcoholic Beverage Program, 17 October 2007, is supplemented as follows: It
outlines Davis-Monthan Air Force Base (DMAFB) procedures to control, sell, and use alcoholic
beverages. It applies to all personnel who sell, serve, purchase, and consume alcohol on
DMAFB. This supplement applies to Air National Guard (ANG) units and United States Air
Force Reserve (USAFR) units assigned/TDY (Temporary Duty) to DMAFB. Ensure that all
records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located in Air Force Records
Information     Management    System     (AFRIMS)      at   Air    Force     (AF)     Portal:
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Contact supporting records managers as
required. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication, through 355 FSS/CC, 5260 East Granite Road, DMAFB, AZ 85707.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Major
changes include:

Unit leadership name and squadron designation. There are also changes to the minimum
possession ages as well as current serving hours, locations and special functions.

1.2. (Added) Changed name of 355 SVS (Services Squadron) to 355 FSS (Force Support
Squadron). Changed name of Officers and Enlisted Club to Mirage Co-located Club (paragraph
1.10).
1.3.2.21. (Added) 21 Years of age is the minimum age for possession of all malt beverages and
alcoholic beverages.
       1.3.1.1. (Added) 19 years of age is the minimum age for selling or serving of all malt
       beverages and alcoholic beverages.

Lodging                                                            Drink        0001-2400
Army Air Force Exchange Service (AAFES) Facilities                 Package      1000-2100


2.1.6.1. The following activities may sell the following alcoholic beverages:
Mirage Co-located Club        Malt Beverages, Distilled Spirits and Wine
Bowling Center Snack Bar      Malt Beverages, Distilled Spirits and Wine
Golf Course Snack Bar         Malt Beverages, Distilled Spirits and Wine
Community Center              Malt Beverages, Distilled Spirits and Wine


2.1.6.2. (Added) In addition to the FSS areas, alcoholic beverages may be consumed on
