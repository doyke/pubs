                     TENTH AIR FORCE (10 AF)
                 COMMUNICATIONS AND INFORMATION
                     Critical Information List (CIL)



       Critical Information is information about friendly activities, intentions, ca-
pabilities or limitations that an adversary needs in order to gain a military, politi-
cal, diplomatic, or technological advantage. Such information, if released to an
adversary prematurely, may prevent or forestall mission accomplishment, reduce
mission effectiveness, or cause loss of lives and/or damage to friendly resources.
Protect the Critical Information listed below.


   Reference to line speed, IP addresses, connection points, or network con-
     nectivity diagrams (classified/unclassified systems)
   Specific video, radio, or security systems
   Specific network vulnerabilities/conditions
   Core facilities, capabilities, and function areas
   Base blueprint and significant upgrades
   Specific contingency plans
   Reference to AFCERT/ACM compliance
   Reference to specific personnel that have access to controlled communica-
     tions area
   Specific call signs and frequencies
   Specific equipment inventory lists
   Reference to keying material
