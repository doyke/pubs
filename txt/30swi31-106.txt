The instruction describes responsibilities and procedures, and sets the standard for curfew hours
on Vandenberg AFB (VAFB). It requires all children under the age of 18, who are not
accompanied by an adult, to be in quarters during curfew hours. It also provides exceptions,
when attending adult sponsored functions or events that end after curfew hours, and disposition
of violators. It applies to all family members and their guest who have children residing with
them or visiting the installation. Refer recommended changes and conflict to 30 SFS/S3SP, 108
Colorado Avenue, Vandenberg AFB CA 93437, on Air Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation
for Change of Publications. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records
and are disposed of in accordance with the AFRIMS Records Disposition Schedule (RDS)
located at https://afrims.amc.af.mil/rds_series.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The title of this publication is updated to Installation Curfew Procedures which replaces Curfew
Procedures. It updates office symbols and organizational name for the 30 Security Forces
Squadron (30 SFS), the Vandenberg Emergency Communication Center (VECC) replaces the
Security Forces Control Center (SFCC).

1. Responsibilities:
    1.1. The 30th Security Forces Squadron (30 SFS) enforces the policy established by this
    instruction.
    1.2. Parents and sponsors of children under the age of 18 will ensure the children for whom
    they are responsible comply with this instruction.
2. Curfew Hours. The curfew on the installation is from 2200 to 0600 Sunday through
Thursday and 2400 to 0600 Friday and Saturday. During this time all children or bona fide
visitors under the age of 18 must be in their quarters or the quarters they are visiting, unless they
are accompanied by an adult.
3. Exceptions to Curfew Hours:
    3.1. Children attending civic events (church, school, etc.) and adult sponsored functions that
    ends after curfew hours are authorized to go from the function directly to their quarters.
    3.2. Children under the age of 18 returning to the installation after curfew hours must go
    from the entry gate directly to their quarters.
4. Disposition of Violators: Any person in violation of this instruction will be detained,
positively identified, and then escorted to their place of residence. If a minor is under the age of
15, the minor will only be left at their residence if a responsible adult is present to accept custody
of the minor. If a minor cannot be delivered to a residence on-base for whatever reason, the
minor will be taken to the VECC until custody can be transferred to a responsible adult or
agency. In all cases, an incident report will be accomplished, and the violator’s parents, legal
guardian, or other responsible adult will be notified.
