AFI 33-329, 1 Mar 1999, is supplement as follows:
1.1. Base Locator Services.
1.1.1.1. During duty hours (0800- 1700), the Base Locator is located in building 230, extension
747-3344. After duty hours, on weekends and holidays, the Base Locator is located in building 117,
extension 747-7051. Inquiries on civilian personnel will be forwarded to the Civilian Personnel Office
(319 MSS/DPC), extension 747-5029.
1.1.1.2. The unit to which the individual is TDY must forward, by memorandum, the name, rank, duty
phone, home phone, and address (if desired), to 319 CS/SCBA (PSC Mail) and 319 SVS/SVML. The
memorandum must include the date TDY personnel will depart the base.
