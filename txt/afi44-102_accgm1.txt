SUBJECT: MAJCOM Guidance Memorandum for AFI 44-102, Medical Care Management

Releasability: There are no releasability restrictions on this publication.

        By Order of the ACC Command Surgeon, this Guidance Memorandum immediately implements
information in AFI 44-102, Medical Care Management. Compliance with the memorandum is
mandatory. To the extent its direction are inconsistent with other Air Force publications, the information
herein prevails, IAW AFI 33-360, Publications and Forms Management.

        AFI 44-102, 20 January 2012, is supplemented as follows: This supplement focuses on the
addition of medical care management standards established by Air Combat Command (ACC) Command
Surgeon (SG) and the responsibilities of medical personnel assigned to ACC to comply with these
standards.

        1.2.3.3. ACC/SG will develop and publish additional medical standards with which all ACC-
assigned medical personnel must comply. Standards will be available on the ACC/SG website at
https://kx.afms.mil/kxweb/dotmil/kj.do?functionalArea=HQACC

       1.2.4.3. ACC MTF/CCs will ensure all personnel are familiar and comply with additional
medical standards.

        The memorandum becomes void after one-year has elapsed from the date of this memorandum,
or upon publication of an Interim Change or re-write of the affected publication, whichever is earlier.
