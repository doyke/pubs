This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply System Research and Records
Maintenance. It applies to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve, and Air Force
Guard organizations supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in contract or
support agreement terms or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management
of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule
(RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Send recommended
changes and questions about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF IMT 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication. This publication requires collecting and
maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and
E.O. 9397. System of records notice F023 AF IL C applies.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The summary of change includes information about the SPS System, what is not required with
maintenance organizations, and information concerning Tyndall AFB Form 14, Organization
Record Load/Change/Delete Request.

27.28.2. The SPS System is not used to maintain local purchase non-NSN description file, a DD
Form 1348-6 file will be used to maintain non-NSN description files.
27.31.1.4. Use DD Form 1348-6 for AFMC managed items.
27.32.5. Serial numbers 000001 - 019999 will be used for L and P serial numbers.
27.32.6. The L & P Serialized Log is used to monitor reuse of serial numbers.
27.36.1. Annual Technical Order reconciliation with maintenance organizations is not required.
27.42.3. Work listings are maintained in electronic format. These listings are updated by filing
output notices for loads, changes, or deletes. Output notices are discarded when new listings are
produced.
27.42.3.2. The D04 is retained in date sequence and stored in electronic format.
27.42.3.4. Program NGV404, AFMC D043B ISG Update, is used to update interchangeable and
substitute (ISG) groups.
27.143.5. Tyndall AFB Form 14, Organization Record Load/Change/Delete Request, is used to
load, change or delete Organization Codes. All Tyndall AFB Form 14s will be forwarded to

325 MSG/LGRD for review. Funds will review, approve and forward Tyndall AFB Form 14 to
Financial Services.
