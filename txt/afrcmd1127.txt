SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication is significantly revised and must be completely reviewed.
1. Mission: The mission of ASTS/ASTF is to train to provide a level of continency capability
sufficient to ensure appropriate supportive and emergency care for patients transiting the Enroute
Care system, including aeromedical evacuation. Additionally, select ASTS/ASTF unit, through
their aerospace medicine capability, provide pre- and post-mobilization medical support to
authorized personnel in support of the parent wing's mission until directed otherwise by
competent authority.
2. Command. The structure maintains effective operations with the least expenditures of
resources and minimizes organizational turbulence when mobilized.
