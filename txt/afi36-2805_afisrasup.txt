Air Force (AF) Instruction (AFI) 36-2805, 29 June 2001 is supplemented as follows. This
supplement establishes the positions in Air Force Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance
Agency (AFISRA) authorized to use appropriated funds to purchase items for recognition and
appreciation. This publication applies to all AFISRA and subordinate unit personnel. This
publication does not apply to AFISRA-gained Air National Guard or Air Force Reserve
members. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Forms 847 from the field through the appropriate functional’s chain of
command. Ensure that all records created as a result of the processes prescribed in this
publication are maintained in accordance with (IAW) AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of IAW the Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS)
Records           Disposition           Schedule            (RDS)             located        at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.         Contact your supporting records
manager as required.

1.4.5. (Added) The following Air Force Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance Agency
(AFISRA) personnel are authorized to use appropriated funds to purchase awards for their On-
the-Spot Recognition and Appreciation Awards Program.
1.4.5.1. (Added) AFISRA Commander.
1.4.5.2. (Added) AFISRA Vice Commander.
1.4.5.3. (Added) AFISRA Command Chief Master Sergeant.
 2                                      AFI36-2805_AFISRASUP 23 SEPTEMBER 2011


1.4.5.4. (Added) AFISRA Center Commanders.
1.4.5.5. (Added) AFISRA Wing Commanders.
1.4.5.6. (Added) AFISRA Center/Wing Command Chief Master Sergeants.
1.4.5.7. (Added) AFISRA Group and Squadron Commanders.
1.4.6. (Added) Appropriated funds may be used to purchase coins, plaques, pens or similar
items if they are used as a form of recognition and will not be used as mementos or gifts
(including going away gifts), nor should they be issued simply for being assigned to an
organization (refer to AFI 65-603, Official Representation Funds, to use funds to purchase
mementos or gifts).
1.4.7. (Added) . These items are only to be used to recognize exceptional mission
accomplishment if appropriated funds are used. They will not be used as mementos or gifts
(including going away gifts), nor should they be issued simply for being assigned to an
organization (refer to AFI65-603, Official Representation Funds, to use funds to purchase
mementos/gifts).
