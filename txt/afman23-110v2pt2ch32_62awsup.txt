AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Part Two, Chap 32, is supplemented as follows: This chapter explains the
actions bases take when the Standard Base Supply System (SBSS) is temporarily inoperative or when
other circumstances impose significant limitations on normal processing capabilities. This supplement is
not applicable to Air National Guard or Air Force Reserve units attached to McChord AFB. Ensure that
all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with
AFMAN 37-123 (will convert to AFMAN 33-363), Management of Records, and disposed of in accor-
dance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://afrims.amc.af.mil/.
Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility
(OPR) using the AF IMT 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF IMT 847s from the
field through the appropriate functional’s chain of command.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Major changes include:
Office symbols and titles were updated to reflect current alignment within the 62d Logistics Readiness
Squadron (LRS). In addition to the mandatory listings in the basic manual, additional post-post listings
were added. Some paragraphs were renumbered to conform to the basic manual. Added Attachment
32C-3 (Added) Glossary of References and Supporting Information. A bar (|) indicates changes since the
last edition.
