5.1.23.2. The Mission Support Group Commander has designated the east gate, located on
Simler Boulevard, as the entry and exit point for petroleum transport vehicles on Columbus
AFB. After the Vehicle Inspection Section (14 SFS) conducts a complete vehicle search of all
petroleum transport vehicles, vehicles will report directly to the Bulk Storage Element, Fuels
Management Flight. Transport vehicles will proceed down Simler Boulevard; take a left turn
 2                                   AFI 23-201CH5_COLUMBUSAFBSUP 24 APRIL 2012


onto Brandt Street behind the Base Exchange and Commissary facilities. Brandt Street leads
transport vehicles directly to Bulk Storage. Personnel at Bulk Storage will inspect each
incoming vehicle IAW DoD 4140.25-M, Vol. II, Chap. 5, and/or DESC Interim Policies and
Procedures. Upon completion, vehicles will exit Bulk Storage turning right onto Brandt Street
and taking the immediate right onto B Place. Vehicles will proceed on B Place, merging right
onto Seventh Street. From Seventh Street; take the left onto Brandt Street and proceed to Simler
Boulevard. Take a right onto Simler Boulevard and exit the installation through the east gate. If
for any reason a deviation from the route is necessary, the Security Forces will be notified.
5.1.23.4. Petroleum transport vehicles will be randomly checked by Fuels Management Quality
Control personnel prior to departing the bulk storage area. Spot checks are conducted to ensure
all tank compartments are empty and release inspections are documented.
