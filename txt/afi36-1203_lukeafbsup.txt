Air Force Instruction 36-1203, Administrative Grievance Systems, is supplemented as follows.
This supplement is applicable to all appropriated fund non-bargaining unit employees serviced
by the Civilian Personnel Office at Luke Air Force Base. It also applies to Air Force Reserve
Command and to Air National Guard units attached to Luke AFB. Refer recommended changes
and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF
Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Forms 847 from the field
through the appropriate functional’s chain of command. Ensure that all records created as a
result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-
363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS). The
authorities to waive wing/unit level requirements in this publication are identified with a Tier
(“T-0, T-1, T-2, T-3”) number following the compliance statement. See AFI 33-360,
Publications and Forms Management, for a description of the authorities associated with the Tier
numbers. This publication may not be supplemented. The use of the name or mark of any
specific manufacturer, commercial product, commodity, or service in this publication does not
imply endorsement by the Air Force.

3.1. (Added) Except as provided in paragraph 3, the deciding official on formal grievances
shall be the employee’s Group Commander or equivalent.
9.1. (Added) Grievance files will be established and maintained by the Civilian Personnel
Office.
10.3.1. (Added) Notification that a grievance is untimely or not covered by the Administrative
Grievance System must be in writing.
10.5.1. (Added) The informal grievance decision must provide the employee with the name
and office symbol of the official who is authorized to make a final decision on the grievance.
11.2.1. (Added) The grievance must be filed through the Civilian Personnel Office (Attn: 56
FSS/FSMC, 7383 N. Litchfield Rd Suite 1172, Luke AFB, AZ 85309-1514), but addressed to the
appropriate group commander or equivalent unless the grievance involves that individual. In that
instance, the grievance will be addressed to the applicable higher level individual or office within
the organizational chain.
11.7.1. (Added) Request for review will be submitted to a deciding official at a higher level
than the official who made the initial decision.
