This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 65-1, Management of Financial
Services. It defines a local commute area as mandated by the Joint Federal Travel Regulation
(JFTR) and applies to military personnel assigned to the 910 Airlift Wing (910AW). Refer all
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility (OPR) using the Air Force Form (AF) 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Form 847 from the field through the appropriate functional’ s chain of
command. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information Management
System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS). NOTE: Any document, form, or
record created using Personal Identification Information (PII), must do so within the scope,
limitations, and protection of Air Force Instruction (AFI) 33-332 “Air Force Privacy Act
Program” and United States Code (U.S.C.) 552a, “Privacy Act of 1974”.


1. Purpose. Establishment of a local commute area is necessary for proper determination of
entitlements when reserve personnel are on active duty orders or while on inactive duty status
(UTA, RMP, AFTP, AGTP). Requests for waivers must be submitted to the OPR listed above
for consideration and approval.
 2                                                            910AWI65-114 2 APRIL 2013


2. Responsibilities. Squadron Commanders have the responsibility to ensure that all Reserve
Orders (RO), AF Form 938, Request and Authorization for Active Duty Training/Active Duty
Tour, or equivalent computer generated orders accurately reflect the commute status of the
individual while performing a tour of active duty.
3. Procedures. Localities classified as being within the commute area of the 910 Airlift Wing,
located at Youngstown Air Reserve Station, Vienna, Ohio, have been determined solely by zip
code and are reflected in Attachment 2. Only the identified zip codes are within the commute
area. City name cannot be used to determine eligibility. Unit orders clerks must utilize this
publication during preparation of AF Form 938 to determine the correct status for reserve
personnel performing active duty.
4. Corporate Limits. The 910 Airlift Wing, Youngstown Air Reserve Station, is located within
Vienna Township, Trumbull County, Ohio, but not within the legal corporate limits of any city.
