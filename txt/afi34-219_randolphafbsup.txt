This publication implements and extends the guidance in AFI 34-219, Alcoholic Beverage
Program, 9 September 1998. This supplement also applies to associate units. Ensure all records
created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with
AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the AF Records
Disposition Schedule (AF RDS) located at: https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61/afrims/afrims.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This supplement updates the 12th SVS facilities on base to current proper designations; removes
12 MSS as an authorized area to consume alcoholic beverages (paragraph 2.1.6.1.); and permits
the consumption of alcoholic beverages in flying training squadrons (paragraph 2.1.6.2.).

1.1.2.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). Alcoholic beverages will not be sold to or consumed by
anyone under 21 years of age on Randolph AFB or the Randolph AFB off-base recreational area.

1.7.2.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). The accounting office will maintain the applicable retail
permit in the respective NAFI life-of-the-fund-file.

2.1.6.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). The following Randolph AFB activities are permitted to sell
alcoholic beverages during established operating hours:

2.1.6.1.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). Enlisted Club—all.

2.1.6.1.2. (Added-Randolph AFB). Officers' Club—all.

2.1.6.1.3. (Added-Randolph AFB) Golf course clubhouse—all.

2.1.6.3. (Added-Randolph AFB). Possession and consumption of beer or wine are authorized at
the following locations on Randolph AFB:

2.1.6.3.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). Eberle Park.

2.1.6.3.2. (Added-Randolph AFB). Hunt and Saddle Club.

2.1.6.3.3. (Added-Randolph AFB). Ball fields (except at youth activities).

2.1.6.4. (Added-Randolph AFB). Units wanting to consume alcoholic beverages in the
workplace for organizational parties/functions will request authorization through the 12
MSG/CD, in turn, at least 5 days before the function. State the date, time, location, and
name/rank of the project officer that the OPR ensures alcoholic beverages are not consumed by
anyone under the age of 21, food and nonalcoholic beverages are available, and there are
designated drivers if needed. The approval authority for organizational requests is also delegated
as follows:

2.1.6.4.1. (Added-Randolph AFB). HQ AETC/DS for all AETC units.

2.1.6.4.2. (Added-Randolph AFB). AFPC/CC for all AFPC units.

2.1.6.4.3. (Added-Randolph AFB). AFSVA/CC for all AFSVA units.

2.1.6.4.4. (Added-Randolph AFB). 19 AF/CV for all units assigned to B661.

2.1.6.4.5. (Added-Randolph AFB). AFCQMI/CV for units assigned to AFCQMI.

2.1.6.4.6. (Added-Randolph AFB). AFRS/CV for units assigned to AFRS.

3.2.2. Clubs are authorized to sell beer, wine coolers, and other commercially packaged
alcoholic beverages with less than 7% alcoholic content by volume for off-premises
consumption, but must restrict sales to times when AAFES-operated retail outlets are closed.
Packaged beer (including kegs or wine) offered as part of a party contract are exempt from the
time restriction.
