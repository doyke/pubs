This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive 44-1, Medical Operations. This
instruction describes the proper completion and submission pre and post operative refractive
surgery forms. This instruction applies to all personnel assigned, attached or under contract to
the 59th Medical Wing (MDW). This instruction does not apply to the Air National Guard or
Air Force Reserve. This publication requires the collection and or maintenance of information
protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 55, Medical and Dental Care, and
E.O. 9397 (SSN). The applicable SORN F044 AF SG D, and Automated Medical/Dental Record
System is available at: http://dpclo.defense.gov/privacy/SORNs/SORNs.htm.                   Refer
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. The
authority to waive requirements is the publication approval authority. Ensure that all records
created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with
(IAW) Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of IAW Air Force
Records Information Management System Records Disposition Schedule.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication has been revised. This rewrite of 59 MDWI 44-188 includes the addition of 2
new pre and post operative refractive surgery forms.
1. Overview.
   1.1. The purpose of 59 MDW Form 196, CRS Pre-Operative Evaluation; 59 MDW Form
   5026, Informed Consent for Excimer Laser Photorefractive Keratectomy; 59 MDW Form

     5025, WHMC Week 1 Post-Operative PRK; 59 MDW Form 195, Aviation and Special Duty
     Personnel Refractive Surgery Post –Op Examination Form; 59 MDW Form 5024,
     Warfighter Refractive Surgery Post-Operative Form is to document before and after patient
     care at the Joint Warfighter Refractive Surgery Center.
2. Personnel Responsibilities.
     2.1. All refractive surgery health care team members are to ensure the forms are
     accomplished and maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of
     Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management
     System             Records            Disposition          Schedule           located
     https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm
3. Forms Submission.
     3.1. 59 MDW Form 196, 59 MDW Form 5026, 59 MDW Form 5025, 59 MDW Form 195,
     59 MDW Form 5024, are maintained indefinitely at the Joint Warfighter Refractive Surgery
     Center. Select data is entered into Armed Forces Health Longitudinal Technology
     Application to facilitate communication with other members of the Air Force Medical
     Service and Department of Defense Health Care teams.
