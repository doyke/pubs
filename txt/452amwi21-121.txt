This Instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 21-1, Air and Space
Maintenance. This AMWI extends the guidance of Air Force Instruction (AFI) 21-101, Aircraft
and Equipment Maintenance Management, AFI 21-101_AFRC_SUP 1, Aircraft and Equipment
Maintenance Management Air Force Instruction, T.O. 00-20-14, Air Force Meteorology and
Calibration, T.O. 33-1-27, Logistics Support of Precision, and T.O. 37C11-1-1, Maintenance
Inst of Pressure Gauges Used on Liquid Oxygen Systems. It will be the responsibility of the 452
AMW Test, Measurement and Diagnostic Equipment representatives to comply with the
guidance and instructions. This AMWI is applicable to all assigned personnel in the 452d Air
Mobility Wing. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office
of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Form 847s from the field through Major Command (MAJCOM)
publications/forms managers. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed
in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition                  Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gessaf61a/afrims/afrims/. The use of the name or mark of any specific
manufacturer, commercial product, commodity or service in this publication does not imply
endorsement by the Air Force.

1. Procedures.
   1.1. Category 2 TMDE:
2                                                     452AMWI21-121 25 SEPTEMBER 2013


       1.1.1. The 412 CRS/TMDE, Edwards AFB, will perform calibration on all category 2
       TMDE.
       1.1.2. Each individual work center is responsible for having their category 2 TMDE
       requiring calibration ready for pickup no later than 0800 hours on the day prior to the
       calibration due date, by the 452 MXS PMEL representative.
       1.1.3. Equipment will be dropped off at the Customers Office on Tuesdays between 0800
       and 1000 hrs by the 452 MXS PMEL representative.
       1.1.4. Complete a 350 tag for each item requiring initial calibration.
       1.1.5. The documentation section from 412 CRS/TMDE, Edwards AFB, will furnish the
       performing work center with an update and a Master ID List, as applicable.
    1.2. Category 3 TMDE:
       1.2.1. Common commercial/military standard TMDE used for maintenance,
       troubleshooting, testing and verification/calibration of mission equipment.
    1.3. Scheduler:
       1.3.1. The TMDE scheduler is appointed from the 412 CRS, TMDE Flight, Edwards
       AFB, CA.
    1.4. Representative:
       1.4.1. The 452 MXS TMDE Representative will be appointed from the 452 MXS/CC
       and will coordinate all transactions with the 412 CRS/TMDE scheduler, to include:
           1.4.1.1. Appointment of a primary and alternate TMDE representative, by
           memorandum.
           1.4.1.2. Establishing hours for the turn-in and/or pick-up of TMDE.
           1.4.1.3. Providing adequate personnel to load TMDE in excess of 75 lbs into the
           delivery vehicle.
           1.4.1.4. Ensuring correct items of equipment are delivered to, and received from the
           scheduler.
           1.4.1.5. Ensuring all listed equipment, for calibration, is receipted by the scheduler.
           1.4.1.6. Notifying owning work center supervision of missing or undelivered items
           due calibration.
           1.4.1.7. Ensuring all transported equipment is properly packaged and transported
           IAW governing directives.
           1.4.1.8. Signing out from the Maintenance Control Center (MCC) a government
           cellular telephone to be used in case of an unforeseen emergency while transporting
           TMDE from March ARB to Edwards AFB.
           1.4.1.9. Coordinating scheduling actions with 452 AMW work center TMDE
           coordinators.
           1.4.1.10. Acting as a liaison between the 452 AMW and 412 CRS/TMDE.

  1.5. Work Center TMDE Coordinators
     1.5.1. Appointed, by memorandum, by the owning work center Flight Chiefs.
     1.5.2. Memorandums of appointment will be submitted to the 452 MXS TMDE
     representative.
     1.5.3. Perform their duties as outlined in the 412 CRS TMDE Flight Coordinators Guide.
     1.5.4. Properly care for assigned TMDE IAW T.O. 00-20-14.
     1.5.5. Check calibration/certification due dates against the MDC equipment master ID
     list provided by the 412 CRS scheduler.
     1.5.6. Circle erroneous entries and enters correct information, in red pencil, above the
     entry.
     1.5.7. Enter additions by providing the equipment’s FSC, ID number, nomenclature and
     five digit serial numbers.
     1.5.8. Submit items for initial certification or unscheduled maintenance to the 452 MXS
     TMDE representative with an attached AFTO Form 350 (see the 412 CRS TMDE flight
     coordinators guide for samples).
     1.5.9. Prepare a memorandum of justification for TMDE requiring emergency calibration
     and make arrangements for delivery to the Precision Measurement Equipment
     Laboratory.
  1.6. Hours of Operation Category 3 TMDE:
     1.6.1. Transportation to Edwards AFB; Monday, 0700 hrs (Wednesday if a holiday falls
     on Monday).
     1.6.2. Drop off equipment to work centers – Tuesday, 0800-1000.
     1.6.3. Pick-up equipment from work centers – Thursday, 0800-1000.
     1.6.4. If Monday is a Holiday, pick-up and drop off will be on Thursday Only of that
     week.
