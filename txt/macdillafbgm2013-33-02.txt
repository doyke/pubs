SUBJECT: MacDill AFB Guidance Memorandum (GM) for Digitizing Records

RELEASABILITY: There are no releasability restrictions on this publication.

1. By Order of the Commander, MacDill AFB, this GM immediately implements: 1) Digitizing
records, converting to Adobe® PDF® format; 2) Digitally signing of all official records unless
required by law to remain in paper format or have a wet signature. As part of this initiative, the
Base Record Staging Area will no longer accept paper records that can be in an electronic format,
are originally in electronic format and can remain in an electronic format, or are part of an
approved electronic records system. Official records that can be converted to electronic format
are identified by Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS), Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS), Table and Rule (T&R), Notes section. If the T&R contains notes
212 and 213, then the records will be converted to electronic records and stored on the O: Drive
under the Office of Records files plan. Compliance with this memorandum is mandatory. To the
extent, its directions are inconsistent with other Air Force publications; the information herein
prevails, IAW AFI33-360, Publications and Forms Management.

2. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained IAW AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of IAW the Air Force
Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS)
located at https://afrims.amc.af.mil/. The use of the name or mark of any specific manufacturer,
commercial product, commodity, or service in this publication does not imply endorsement by
the Air Force.

3. This memorandum becomes void after one-year has elapsed from the date of this
memorandum, or upon publication of an Interim Change or rewrite of the affected publication,
whichever is earlier.
