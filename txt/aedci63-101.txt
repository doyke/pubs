This instruction implements AFPD 63-1, Integrated Life Cycle Management. It identifies items
and services that are acquired by the AEDC Installation Contracting Division (AFTC/PZI) and
those that are acquired by the AFTC Contract Management Division (AFTC/PZZ) through the
AEDC test operations and mission support contractor. It describes the procedure for submitting
requirements to AFTC/PZI and AFTC/PZZ.

Ensure that all records created as result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained
in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed
of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-afa/afrims/afrims/. The
Operating Contractor will follow records management requirements in accordance with the
contract. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF IMT 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF IMT 847 through the appropriate functional chain of command.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES: This revision is to make minor administrative changes.

1. Policy
1. 1. AFTC/PZI normally acquires the following:
       1.1.1. Any item or group of items for which the estimated cost of the single purchase or
       annual lease from one source exceeds $25,000.
 2                                                             AEDCI63-101 8 AUGUST 2013


        1.1.2. Research and development to be performed by any contractor except the support
        contractor.
        1.1.3. Architect-Engineer services and military construction projects not procured by the
        United States Army Corps of Engineers.
        1.1.4. Utilities
        1.1.5. Education services for Air Force personnel
        1.1.6. Other items that for reasons of propriety or economics should be procured directly
        by the Government.
     1.2. AFTC/PZZ normally acquires the following through or from the test operations and
     mission support contractor:
        1.2.1. Any items or grouping of items for which the estimated value of the single
        purchase or annual lease from one source is $25,000 or less except those listed in
        Paragraph 1.1.
        1.2.2. Any items or group of items for which a stock level is maintained regardless of
        value.
        1.2.3. Research and development for which the operating contractor is the source.
        1.2.4. Sales operations for timber and salvage.
        1.2.5. Emergency purchases to protect human life or the Complex’s testing capability
        provided; however, the operating contractor shall obtain the approval of the Chief,
        AFTC/PZZ, before making any such acquisition in excess of $25,000.
        1.2.6. Maintenance of automatic data processing equipment (ADPE) and software.
        1.2.7. Consultant services acquired by the operating contractor that are necessary to
        support accomplishments of the Performance Work Statement (PWS).
     1.3. Exceptions to the policies specified herein may be granted only by the Director of
     Contracting (AFTC/PZ).
2. Procedure
     2.1. Requirements initiated by AEDC offices for acquisition by AFTC/PZ are processed as
     follows:
        2.1.1. The requiring organization provides AFTC/PZI with a complete procurement
        package and the required funding (AF Form 9, Request for Purchase, or AFMC Form 36,
        Purchase Request) through the Financial Management and Comptroller Directorate
        (AEDC/FM).
     2.2. Requirements approved for acquisition through the test operations and mission support
     contractor are processed as follows:
        2.2.1. The requiring organization provides AFTC/PZZ with a complete procurement
        package and required funding (AFMC Form 36) thru AEDC/FM.

     2.2.2. Exceptions to the policy set forth in Paragraph 1, with respect to which AFTC/PZ
     Division conducts the acquisition, may be approved by AFTC/PZ. Requests for
     exceptions or waiver of the policy may be submitted by formal letter or electronically to
     AFTC/PZ.
