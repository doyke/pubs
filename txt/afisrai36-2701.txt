This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive 36-27, Equal Opportunity (EO). It also
prescribes Air Force Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance Agency (AFISRA) Visual
Aid 36-2701, The Federal Equal Employment Opportunity Discrimination Complaint Process
for the Air Force Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance Agency (AFISRA). It should be
used with the policies and guidance published by the Equal Employment Opportunity
Commission (EEOC) in Title 29, Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), Parts 1614.102, 1614.105
and 1614.106; EEOC Management Directive 110; and applicable statutes. This instruction
applies to AFISRA units worldwide, and AFISRA-gained Air Force Reserve and Air National
Guard units. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Forms 847 from the field through the appropriate functional’s chain of
command. This publication may be supplemented at any level, but all direct supplements must
be routed through the OPR prior to certification and approval. Maintain records created as a
result of the prescribed processes identified in this directory in accordance with (IAW) AFMAN
33-363, Management of Records, and dispose of them IAW the AF Records Disposition
Schedule        (RDS)       found      on       the      Air      Force      Portal    link  at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact supporting records managers
as required.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication was revised due to change/conversion of the prescribing directive from 36-12 to
36-27. All other changes were administrative in nature and did not change a process or content.
1. In order to implement its EO program, each activity shall:
     1.1. Make written materials available to all employees and applicants informing them of the
     variety of equal employment opportunity programs and administrative and judicial remedial
     procedures available to them.
     1.2. Prominently post such written materials in all personnel and EO offices and throughout
     the workplace [29 CFR 1614.102 (b) (4)].
     1.3. As a minimum, locally developed EO posters will include the names and contact
     information for EO Counselors.
2. AFISRAVA 36-2701 provides guidance and procedures for managing the informal (pre-
complaint) and formal complaint processes for AFISRA personnel. All AFISRA staff
offices and AFISRA worldwide subordinate organizations will publicize AFISRAVA 36-2701 to
their employees.
     2.1. Current copies of AFISRAVA 36-2701 will be posted at the workplace, accessible to
     assigned personnel.
