This supplement implements and extends guidance of Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, V2, PT13
and AFMAN 23-110,V2, PT13, AFSPC SUP1. This supplement describes 45 SW procedures for use in
conjunction with the basic AFMAN and AFSPC Supplement. It applies to all 45 SW activities, tenants
and contractors operated or supported by the USAF Standard Base Supply System. This supplement does
apply to Air Force Reserve Command and Air National Guard units.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This 45 SW supplement to AFMAN 23-110, V2, PT13, reflects minor change.

AFMAN 23-110, (CD) BASIC, Volume 2, Part 13, Standard Base Supply Customer's Procedures is
supplemented as follows:

CHAPTER 6

SECTION A
6.11.2. AF Form 2001, Notification of TCTO Kits Requirements, will be submitted in three copies along
with one copy of the applicable technical order. Delivery instructions will be furnished on each
submission of AF Form 2001. Repair Cycle Support office will retain one copy of AF Form 2001 citing a
requisition number the material was ordered on.
