This publication implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 32-10, Installations and
Facilities. It establishes procedures to permit access to certain rooms on Laughlin AFB and
applies to individuals at all levels who have access or want to gain access to certain
rooms/buildings. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office
of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management
System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61a/afrims/afrims/.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication has been updated to comply with formatting and coordination requirements in
AFI 33-360, Publications and Forms Management.
1. This instruction establishes local procedures to permit access by other than qualified
Civil Engineer personnel to certain boiler rooms on Laughlin AFB.
2. Authorized Access: All mechanical/boiler rooms for all facilities on base are designated ―off
limits‖, and access is prohibited to other than qualified Civil Engineer personnel with the
following exceptions:

3. Telephone Company Access: It is recognized that the current telephone contractor
maintenance personnel have access to all mechanical rooms for telephone repair purposes only.
4. No one, except as identified in paragraph 2, may start, stop, adjust or tamper with any
facility heating or air conditioning equipment.
5. No one (listed here or not) may use any electrical equipment room for storage.
6. Contractors may only enter mechanical rooms and equipment rooms when authorized
by the 47th Contracting Squadron (extension 298-5117) or by the 47th Civil Engineer
Squadron. Contractors must first sign out the appropriate room key from the Energy
Management Controls Systems (EMCS), extension 298-5398, office located in Building 121.
