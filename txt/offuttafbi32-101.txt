This instruction outlines responsibilities and procedures required to establish the Offutt Air Force
Base Facilities Board (FB). It applies to all voting and nonvoting members of the FB. Ensure
that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/.
Contact supporting records managers as required. Send suggested comments, questions, and
improvements to the publication on an AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication, through 55 CS/SCXKP, 201 Lincoln Highway, Suite 206, Bldg 41, Offutt Air Force
Base, Nebraska, 68113.

1. Offutt AFB FB Membership. The 55th Wing Commander is the Base FB Chairperson. The
FB is permanently comprised of voting and nonvoting representatives as follows:
   1.1. Voting Members:
       1.1.1. 55 WG Commander, Chairperson
       1.1.2. 55 WG Vice Commander
       1.1.3. 55 OG Commander
       1.1.4. 55 MXG Commander
       1.1.5. 55 MSG Commander
       1.1.6. 55 CG Commander

        1.1.7. 55 MDG Commander
        1.1.8. Base Civil Engineer
     1.2. Nonvoting Members: All associate unit commanders.
2. The FB will validate the prioritization of presented projects. These projects are
developed primarily by the 55 CES Programming Element in priority order and are then given to
the FB for validation. The validated project priorities will be updated in the Automated Civil
Engineer System – Project Management (ACES-PM).
     2.1. The FB reviews and approves:
        2.1.1. Project Priority
        2.1.2. Project Fiscal Year
        2.1.3. The Military Construction Program
        2.1.4. Real Property Transactions
        2.1.5. Use of Existing Facilities
        2.1.6. Acquisition and Use of Temporary Facilities
        2.1.7. Request for Proprietary Approval
     2.2. The FB reviews other items deemed appropriate by the chairperson.
3. Procedures:
     3.1. A Pre-Facilities Board will convene each quarter, and will be chaired by the 55th
     Mission Support Group Commander or the commander’s designated representative. The
     purpose of the Pre-Facilities Board is to discuss the topics as found in paragraph 2.1. The
     Group Commander designated representative(s) should be knowledgeable of project(s) being
     present for their Group at the functional level and be able to make recommendations to the
     Pre-FB. Attendee(s) should then brief their Group Commander of the results of the Pre-FB.
     3.2. The FB will meet each quarter, and will be chaired by the 55th Wing Commander or the
     commander’s representative.
     3.3. The chairperson of the FB may call for a meeting at any time.
     3.4. The Chief of the Engineer Flight or the designated representative will be the recorder
     and will publish minutes of the FB actions and decisions and identify the Office of Primary
     Responsibility (OPR) for open action items as required.
