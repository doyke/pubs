AFI 32-6001, 21 August 2006 is supplemented as follows. This Seymour Johnson AFB
(SJAFB) Supplement to AFI 32-6001 defines Key and Essential (K&E) housing requirements for
SJAFB. This Supplement applies to active duty incumbents of the K&E positions as identified
by the 4FW/CC. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this
publication are maintained IAW Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of IAW Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS). Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication
to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR).

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. This
publication (1) identifies housing units for designated K&E personnel, and (2) identifies house
addresses and assignment priority of Chief Prestige housing units. House numbers and street
names have changed for all K&E houses. Seven additional houses were added for FGO K&E
personnel.
2.20.2.1. 4 FW/CC, 4 FW/CV, 4 FW Group Commanders, 4 FW Group Deputy Commanders, 4
FW Squadron Commanders, 4 FW/JA, 911 ARS Commander, Base Chaplain, Command
CMSgt, and 4 FW Group CMSgts are identified as key and mission
