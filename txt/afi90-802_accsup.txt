AFI 90-802 is supplemented as follows. This publication authorizes the use of ACC Form 167,
ACC Risk Management (RM) Worksheet, for standardization and use throughout Air Combat
Command (ACC). It is a comprehensive worksheet that will guide any RM activity through all
five (5) steps of the RM process. It does apply to ACC gained AF Reserve Command (AFRC)
and Air National Guard (ANG) units or members. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this supplement are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS). Contact supporting
records managers as required. We welcome your comments and recommendations on ways to
better improve this product. Send comments and suggested improvements on AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, through channels to HQ ACC/SE, 220 Sweeney
Blvd, Ste 308, JB Langley-Eustis VA 23665-2714. This publication may be supplemented at
any level, but all direct supplements must be routed to the OPR of this publication for
coordination prior to certification and approval.

    4.10.3.2. (Added) The ACC Form 167, ACC Risk Management (RM) Worksheet,
    may be used to identify, assess, and document safety hazards and deliberate risk
    assessments.
    4.10.4.1. (Added) RM Advisors in units with a flying mission will ensure that pre-
    flight RM assessment worksheets comply with current ACC/A3 requirements, and
    that RM trend data is reviewed at quarterly aircrew flying safety meetings and
    provided to OGV.
    4.10.4.2. (Added) RM trend analysis may include individual or overall RM
    assessment scores, mission element scores, and a review of the risk acceptance
    threshold values, to ensure thresholds meet mission and unit commander
    requirements. Other elements for analysis will be included at the discretion of the
    unit commander or the RM Advisor.
