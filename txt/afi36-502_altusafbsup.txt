This supplement implements and extends the guidance of AFI 36-502, Managing Civilian
Personnel Resources, 30 Mar 94. It establishes the 97th Air Mobility Wing Civilian
Employment Cost Management Committee (CECMC) to review civilian strength and cost data.
This instruction applies to all 97 AMW units. Refer recommended changes and questions about
this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using Air Force (AF) Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847 from the field through the
appropriate functional’s chain of command. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this document are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, are disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact supporting records managers
as required.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document has been substantially revised and must be reviewed in its entirety. This
publication establishes the 97th Air Mobility Wing CECMC to review civilian strength and cost
data, per AFI 36-502, Managing Civilian Personnel Resources.

AFI 36-502, 30 March 1994, is supplemented as follows:
2.2.1. (Added) Civilian Employment Cost Management Committee (CECMC) Composition.
97 AMW CECMC is chaired by the Manpower and Personnel Flight Chief. Committee
members are the Director of Staff, deputy group commanders, AMW Comptroller, and
representatives from manpower and civilian personnel.
2.2.2. (Added) CECMC Responsibilities. 97 AMW CECMC meetings will be scheduled by
FSS/FSM each quarter if needed. Ad hoc meetings may also be scheduled if needed for time
sensitive requirements. 97 AMW CECMC members will discuss and resolve resource
management issues pertaining to:
2.2.2.1. (Added) Workyear utilization.
2.2.2.2. (Added) Civilian Funding.
2.2.2.3. (Added) Overhire, temporary, part-time, authorizations/expenditures, etc.
2.2.2.4. (Added) Upgrade requests prior to classification.
2.2.2.5. (Added) Other concerns which may affect 97 AMW civilian funding, personnel, or
manpower authorization allocation issues.
2.2. 3 (Added) CECMC Process. Deputy group commanders will forward documents
requesting overhires and upgrades (using templates at Attachments 2 and 3) to the CECMC chair
prior to the committee meeting. The Chair will forward documents to all members for review
and collaboration with group commanders. The Director of Staff (DS) will confer with the Wing
Commander for his vector. Deputy Commanders and the DS will come to the meeting with their
commander’s proxy for issues being discussed. Group consensus will be reflected in the
CECMC minutes. The CECMC chair will brief final recommendations at the quarterly Financial
Management Board meeting for 97 AMW/CC approval to implement. Ad hoc meeting
recommendations will be presented by the CECMC chair to Group and Wing leadership via a
staff package or briefing.

                                        Attachment 1
         GLOSSARY OF REFERENCES AND SUPPORTING INFORMATION

Terms
Civilian Pay Budget—The total obligation authority comprised of Direct Obligating Authority
dollars and earnings from reimbursements.
Civilian Resource Management—The practice of balancing mission needs, operational
economy and efficiency, skills and career paths, employee development and motivation, and
recruitment and retention of competent people within a civilian pay budget.
Employment Planning—Identifying employment levels and work years needed to accomplish
the mission within the civilian pay budget.
Resource Allocation Process—The Air Force corporate approach to planning, programming,
and budgeting the resources needed to execute Air Force programs now and for the next 6 years.
The process uses six functional teams who develop options and make recommendations to senior
leadership.
Civilian Personnel Resources—The dollars allocated to an organization for the purpose of
civilian employment.
