AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 16, is supplemented as follows: This publication
applies to all personnel assigned/attached to Shaw AFB, South Carolina. Ensure that all records
created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with
Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance
with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact
supporting records managers as required. Refer recommended changes and questions about this
publication to 20 LRS/LGLOC. 321 Cullen St., Shaw AFB, SC 29152-5125; route through your
appropriate functional chain of command.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

16.2.1. An RVP control log (RVP LOG.mdb) will be used by each respective section processing
the RVP. This log can be accessed on the Logistics Readiness Squadron (LRS) Local Area
Network (LAN) “S:\” drive, under “RVP LOG,” “RVP,” “RVP forms.” Each respective section
processing an RVP will enter the name of the person responsible for the error in the Reverse Post
Log block entitled “RVP Explanation.”
16.4.1. Due to decentralization of Inventory processes; a Non-Commissioned Officers (NCOs)
or civilian equivalent, is responsible for initiating and processing RVPs for their respective
warehouse only after receiving approval from flight commander, chief and/or superintendent.

Personnel authorized to process Transaction Identifier Code (TRIC) RVP will update the RVP
log with all correct information prior to processing the RVP.
16.7. Preparation of Reverse-Post Input. 20 LRS Form 5, 20th Logistics Readiness Squadron
Reverse Post Request, will be used to document all reverse post actions/requests. This form is
located on the LRS LAN “S:\” drive, under “RVP LOG,” RVP,” RVP Forms.” The requester of
the reverse post will prepare one copy of the 20 LRS Form 5. Attach one copy of the
Consolidated Transaction History (CTH) inquiry, with annotated action taken, to the 20 LRS
Form 5. Each section processing reverse-post inputs will keep a separate file of completed 20
LRS Form 5.
     16.11.2. The Standard Asset Tracking System (SATS) Identification (ID) of the original
     transaction will be annotated on the RVP in addition to the transaction date and transaction
     serial number.
       16.11.3.1. (Added) The original transaction in SATS will be moved to History with the
       words “Reverse Post,” the reason for the RVP, and the RVP transaction number. The RVP
       will be entered in the RVP log.
