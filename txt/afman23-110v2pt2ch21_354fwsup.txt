This supplement is implementing AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 21, Special
Logistics Support Procedures. This supplement will be used in conjunction with AFMAN 23-
110. This supplement does not apply to the Air National Guard or Air Force Reserve units and
members. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with AF Manual AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and
disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil.afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication has been substantially revised and must be thoroughly reviewed.

AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 21 is supplemented as follows:
21.141.2. Follow the hazardous waste disposal procedures outlined in the Eielson AFB
Hazardous Material and Waste Management Plan and in 354FWI 32-7005. Contact the Civil
Engineering Environmental Flight to obtain the Hazardous Material and Waste Management
Plan.

21.141.3. Hazardous material found in stock and determined to be unserviceable will be
removed from the item record by processing an FCC in ES-S to change asset condition code to
“H”. Disposal turn-in documents (A5J) will be outputted as a result. The A5J(s) and the
property will be turned over to Civil Engineering Environmental Flight for disposal in
accordance with 354FWI 32-7005 and the Eielson AFB Hazardous Material and Waste
Management Plan.
21.168.2. Equipment Element will be the point of contact for referral inquiries regarding
Communication Security (COMSEC) serial number reports and data.
