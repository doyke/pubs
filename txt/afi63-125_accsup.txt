Air Force Instruction (AFI) 63-125 is supplemented as follows: It implements policy on
Nuclear Certified Equipment (NCE) Program to ACC wings. This supplement applies to
military and civilian personnel at all levels, including Air Force Reserve and Air National Guard
(ANG) units, involved in research, design, development, testing, acquisition, operation,
maintenance, and modification of nuclear weapons and their related systems and subsystems. It
applies to ACC wings which operate, maintain, acquire, sustain, or modify specialized and non-
specialized equipment/vehicles listed in the Master Nuclear Certification List (MNCL) at
https://wwwmil.nwc.kirtland.af.mil/MNCL/index.cfm. Ensure that all records created as a
result of this publication are in accordance with (IAW) AFMAN 33-363, Management of
Records, and dispose of them IAW the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule. Contact
supporting records managers as required. Send comments and suggested improvements, for this
publication, through channels using AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication,
to HQ ACC/A10 Strategic Deterrence & Nuclear Integration Directorate. This supplement may
be further supplemented.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially changed and must be completely reviewed. A new chapter has
been added identifying items that are required to be nuclear certified before being used with
nuclear weapons or nuclear weapon systems. A new chapter has been added describing the
Master Nuclear Certification List (MNCL) and the criteria for listing items in the MNCL.
