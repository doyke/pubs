1. Mission. The Air Force Review Boards Agency (AFRBA) acts for the Secretary of the Air Force in
deciding cases before the Air Force Personnel Council, the Board for the Correction of Military Records
and the Civilian Appellate Review Office. This organization is a field operating agency of the Office of
the Secretary of the Air Force.

2. Command. The Deputy for Air Force Review Boards:
   2.1. Is responsible to the Assistant Secretary of the Air Force for Manpower, Reserve Affairs, Instal-
   lations and Environment (SAF/MI).
   2.2. Directs the operations of the organizations within the AFRBA.

3. Responsibilities. The Deputy for Air Force Review Boards:
   3.1. Acts for the Secretary of the Air Force in deciding individual cases before the various organiza-
   tions.
   3.2. Renders final decisions for the Secretary of the Air Force regarding remissions of indebtedness
   for enlisted members.
   3.3. Ensures due process, equity and fair and impartial treatment for all Air Force members and civil-
   ian employees.
