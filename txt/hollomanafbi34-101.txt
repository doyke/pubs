This publication establishes procedures for starting times and applies to all customers and
employees of the Apache Mesa Golf Course in accordance with Air Force Instruction (AFI) 34-
101, Air Force Morale, Welfare. and Recreation (MWR) Programs and Use Eligibility, Table
A2.1-A2.2. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management
System      (AFRIMS)       Records      Disposition       Schedule      (RDS)       located      at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact supporting records managers
as required. Refer recommended changes and questions to the Office of Primary Responsibility
(OPR) using AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication, to 49th Force Support
Squadron Apache Mesa Golf Course (49 FSS/FSCG), 151 West Gate Avenue, Holloman AFB,
NM 88330-8078.

1. Terms Explained:
     1.1. The starting times are available Sunday thru Saturday and may be obtained as follows:
        1.1.1. Tee times for single groups may be reserved no earlier than 14 days in advance by
        active duty personnel and 10 days by retired/civilian users. Tee times may be reserved by
        calling the pro shop at 572-3574. Day to day times generally are on a first come first
        served basis. Apache Mesa Golf Course reserves the right to offer advanced tee times
        under these guide lines.
        1.1.2. Groups requesting a block of times can reserve these spots in advance starting in
        January, and then throughout the calendar year. Groups of 36-72 will be considered for a
        shotgun start. Open play during these outings will begin upon completion of the event.
        1.1.3. Twilight play will begin at 1300 during the offseason (Oct – Mar), and 1500
        during the season (Apr – Sep). Twilight fees will be charged at the nine hole rate for
        green fee/cart fee. Twilight tee times are on a first come first serve basis.
        1.1.4. Generally, golf groups must consist of at least three golfers. Pro Shop employees
        will work to keep from filling the course with singles and twosomes during high volume
        play.
2. Waiting List Procedures.
     2.1. The Pro Shop will maintain a waiting list of players who wish to play and do not have a
     tee time and or group to join. Players from the waiting list will be paired with others or given
     a time of their own depending upon situation and number of players.
     2.2. Golfers/groups with reserved times must check in with the pro shop 15 minutes prior to
     their scheduled tee time(s). All players must present their ID cards and or Annual Green Fee
     card upon checking in.
