This instruction establishes standard procedures and responsibilities for the purchase, control, usage, and
presentation of Wing Commander and Command Chief Master Sergeant spot recognition coins within the
31st Fighter Wing in conjunction with USAFE policy and AFPD 36-28, Awards and Decorations Pro-
grams. It applies to all personnel assigned or attached to the 31 FW. Maintain and dispose of resulting
records in accordance with AFMAN 37-123, Management of Records and the Air Force Records Disposi-
tion Schedule located in Air Force Records Information Management (AFRIMs) at https://
afrims.amc.af.mil. Protect list of recipients and any other resulting personal information in accordance
with the Privacy Act of 1974.


1. GENERAL: The purpose of these coins is to provide senior leadership with an informal means to
show special recognition and appreciation for a job well done. Recipients receive immediate, on the spot,
recognition for acts and accomplishments. This is not to replace normal recognition venues, but instead,
to provide an additional opportunity and tool to recognize the superb work of our deserving personnel.

2. ELIGIBILITY: The 31 FW spot recognition coins will be presented by the Wing Commander or
Command Chief Master Sergeant to military and civilian personnel who, by their dedicated individual
effort, have facilitated the successful accomplishment of a particular program, function, or mission. All
recipients must have exhibited sincere dedication, time, and energy in the performance of their duties or
activities meriting immediate recognition, as determined by the presentation officials.

3. RESPONSIBILITIES:
   3.1. 31 FW/CC and CCM will provide spot recognition coins to individuals who merit such recogni-
   tion as deemed appropriate.
   3.2. 31 FW/CCE will maintain, account for, and track coin distribution.
   3.3. 31 FW/CCEA will budget for, and obtain necessary funds to support this program.
