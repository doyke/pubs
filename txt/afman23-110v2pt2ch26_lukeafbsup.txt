AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 26, 1 July 2011, War Reserve Materiel, is
supplemented as follows. This publication does not apply to Air Force Reserve Command or Air
National Guard units. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the
Office of Primary Responsibility using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication, route AF Forms 847 from the field through the appropriate functional’s chain of
command. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with the Air Force Records Information Management System Records Disposition
Schedule located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Individual
Protective Equipment will no longer use Luke AFB Form 302, Mobility Sizing Form.
26.1.1.Changes titles, work centers and responsibilities due to the reorganization of the 56th
Logistics Readiness Squadron (56 LRS). Refer to AFMAN 23-110,Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 2,
Paragraph 2.1.1, Luke AFB Supplement 1, for current work center titles.

*26.13.The 56 LRS Accountable Officer will have Aircraft Parts Store accomplish a complete
inventory when a War Reserve Materiel kit or package is received on transfer or loan.

26.24.5.1.The Temporary Mission Support Kit/Permanent Mission Support Kit (TMSK/PMSK)
assets will be located in a temporary hold area by shop code and detail number sequence for easy
location of assets until they are binned for transportation. When assets are inventoried for pre-
deployment inventory, annotate the bin location on the Mission Support Listing (R50),
TMSK/PMSK, DISCOVERER, and/or equivalent listing for ease in locating assets while
deployed.

26.26.2.2.Use R50 listing, DISCOVERER, and/or equivalent listing and AF Form 2413, Supply
Control Log, for manual accounting during exercises and short-term deployments.

26.26.2.3.3.The R50 listing, DISCOVERER, and/or equivalent listing will be reviewed,
validated, and signed by the maintenance officer and returned to the Aircraft Parts Store prior to
deployments.

26.26.2.3.3.1. (Added)Physical signature or email confirmation will be retained as proof of
validation.
