This instruction implements AFPD 36-30, Military Entitlements; it replaces MAXWELLAFBI
36-3001 that is rescinded. It outlines instructions for completing Maxwell AFB Forms the
Airman and Family Readiness Center uses in its daily operations. Submit an AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, to the OPR to recommend changes to this
publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information Management
System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61a/afrims/afrims/. It applies to all Maxwell-Gunter active duty members and other
personnel with deployment, extended temporary duty (TDY), or remote assignments.

1. MAXWELLAFB Form 192, Family Readiness Deployment Checklist.
   1.1. Policy. The Family Readiness Coordinator offers a MAXWELLAFB Form 192 to
   deploying personnel during their Pre-Deployment Briefing. Unit Commander’s Support
   Staffs may also give MAXWELLAFB Form 192 to their personnel to ensure personal
   readiness at all times.
   1.2. Description. The MAXWELLAFB Form 192 is a checklist of personal documents,
   property and resident information, important telephone numbers, service agreements, and
   child and pet care instructions. It is designed to allow each member to fill in their specific
   personal information in each category.
   1.3. Procedures. Members are encouraged to complete the MAXWELLAFB Form 192
   before departing for their TDY or remote assignment. Members should store their completed

     MAXWELLAFB Form 192 along with all other important documents in a protected place of
     their choice.
2. MAXWELLAFB Form 382, Maxwell-Gunter Airman and Family Readiness Center
Customer Information Sheet.
     2.1. Policy. The Airman and Family Readiness Center staff gives a MAXWELLAFB Form
     382 to customers prior to service. Customer will complete the form and turn it in to a staff
     member so the information can be put into the Air Force Family Integrated Results and
     Statistical Tracking (AFFIRST) program.
     2.2. Description. The MAXWELLAFB Form 382 is a form used to gather information used
     for tracking service, counseling, deployments, and family information. The form is also used
     to input information into the mandatory AFFIRST system.
     2.3. Procedures. The MAXWELLAFB Form 382 must be completed by the member. The
     form will be filed in the active file for one year from the date of the member’s last visit. It
     will then be moved to the inactive file for one year before being shredded.
3. Forms.
     3.1. Adopted: AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication.
     3.2. Prescribed: Maxwell AFB Form 192, Family Readiness Deployment Checklist;
     Maxwell AFB Form 382, Maxwell-Gunter Airman and Family Readiness Center Customer
     Information Sheet.
