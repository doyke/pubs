This instruction sets forth the policies and procedures for scheduling and utilizing the Offutt
AFB B-17 Ceremony Area, Parade Ground Gazebo and Parade Ground Reviewing Stand.
Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed
of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/.
Contact supporting records managers as required. Send suggested comments, questions, and
improvements to the publication on an AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication, through 55 CS/SCXKP, 201 Lincoln Highway, Suite 206, Bldg 41, Offutt Air Force
Base, Nebraska, 68113.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Deleted paragraph reference to airfield observation deck since it was torn down. Changed
references to CCP and Support Group to Mission Support Group.
1. POLICY. The B-17 Ceremony Area, Parade Ground Gazebo, and Parade Ground Reviewing
Stand provide sites for ceremonies, parades, and functions--official and private--for units
associated with the Department of Defense, active duty and retired military personnel, and
civilians employed at Offutt Air Force Base. There will not be any tent stakes or poles set up
without prior coordination with 55th Civil Engineering Squadron at 294-5501. Driving on the
Parade Ground except for mowing purposes is prohibited. The 55th Mission Support Group
Commander is the approval authority for all functions conducted at these three sites.

2. SCHEDULING. All functions and practices will be scheduled through 55th Mission Support
Group Command Section.
3. PROCEDURES FOR RESERVING.
     3.1. All request will be made through 55 MSG/CCS at 294-5552 to reserve the desired site.
55 MSG/CCS will record the POC’s name, office symbol, and phone number.
     3.2. Reservations will be taken on a space available basis. No reservations will be accepted
     for any of the three areas during the annual Offutt Air Show/Open House.
4. RESPONSIBILITIES.
     4.1. 55 MSG/CCS will maintain the schedule for the B-17 Ceremony Area, Gazebo, and
     Reviewing Stand.
     4.2. The host/project officer will be responsible for inspecting the reserved facility to ensure
     it is ready for use. The host/project officer will notify the 55th Mission Support Group at
     294-5552 of any situation needing attention.
     4.3. The maintenance, upkeep, and repair on these sites will be conducted as follows (with
     the assistance from the 55th Mission Support Group, as requested):
        4.3.1. 55th Maintenance Group: B-17 aircraft.
        4.3.2. 55th Mission Support Group: Gazebo and Reviewing Stand.
     4.4. The host/project officer is responsible for activities conducted at the reserved site. This
     responsibility includes making sure the reserved sited is cleaned following the scheduled
     event.
5. Forms Prescribed: None.
