This supplement applies to all 18th Wing and associate units at Kadena Air Base serviced by the 18th
Mission Support Squadron, Civilian Personnel Flight. This publication does not apply to the Air National
Guard or US Air Force Reserve.

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS
This revision updates and clarifies the approval authority for various awards. New or revised material is
indicated by an asterisk (*).
AFI 36-1001, 1 July 1999, is supplemented as follows:
2.7.6.1. (Added) (18 WG). The final approval authority for Performance Awards and Quality Step
Increases is the 18th Wing Commander, group commanders, squadron commanders, and commanders/
commanding officials of associate units at Kadena Air base. This authority may not be further delegated.
