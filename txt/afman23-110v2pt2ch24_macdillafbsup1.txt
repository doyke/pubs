AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chap 24, is supplemented as follows:

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS
This revision adds “The Flightline Service Center Element” in paragraph 24.13.; deletes paragraph
24.19.1.; supplements paragraph 24.19.2.; and changes paragraph 24.38. to read “Buildup Supply points
will be operated by Maintenance.” A bar ( | ) indicates new or revised material since previous edition.
24.13. The Flightline Service Center Element will be the turn-in point for all DIFM assets including
Found On Base (FOB) items. Property turned in to the Flightline Service Center Element will have the
following: Two Material Condition Tags/Labels, a copy of DD Form 1348-1, DOD Single Line Item
Release/Receipt Document, issue/due-out release document or the SATS ID label, reusable container or
AF Form 451, Request for Packaging Service, AFTO Form 350, Repairable Item Processing Tag, if
required, and AFTO Form 20, Caution and Inspection Record, if required, or appropriate historical data
record.
24.29.2. The property and turn-in document will be delivered to the Flightline Service Center Element for
turn in processing.
24.38. Buildup Supply points will be operated by Maintenance.
