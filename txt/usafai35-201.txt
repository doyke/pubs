This instruction implements AFPD 35-2, Public Communications Program, by identifying procedures in
completing USAFA Form 61, Unit Performance Request, and USAFA Form 62, Request for Speaker.
Reference AFI 35-201, Chapters 3 and 4, for background and authority. This instruction applies to mili-
tary and civilian personnel permanently or temporarily assigned to the USAF Academy.
This instruction requires collecting and maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974
authorized by Title 10, United States Code, Section 8013. EO 9397 System of Records Notice F162 AF
SG A, Privacy Act Request File, applies.



1. Procedures. Community Relations (HQ USAFA PAC) processes all requests for cadet performing
units (Honor Guard for retirements, Sabre Drill Team, Drum and Bugle Corps, Cadet Choirs, Falconers,
Wings of Blue, etc.) by submitting USAFA Form 61. HQ USAFA PAC also coordinates requests for
guest speakers for functions on and offbase through the USAFA Speakerâ€™s Bureau. All Academy military
and civilian employees may volunteer to speak by submitting USAFA Form 62. These forms are elec-
tronic and located on the Academy website. Questions regarding filling out the forms can be directed to
HQ USAFA/PA.

2. Forms Prescribed. USAFA Form 61, Unit Performance Request, and USAFA Form 62, Request
for Speaker.
