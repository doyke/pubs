This supplement implements Air Force Instruction (AFI) 21-101, Aircraft and Equipment
Maintenance Management, 29 June 2006 and AFI 21-101_Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC)
Supplement (SUP), Aircraft and Equipment Maintenance Management, 3 February 2007. It
provides guidance and procedures for Hot Refueling for 442d Fighter Wing (442 FW) A-10
Aircraft at Whiteman Air Force Base (AFB). It applies to all personnel of the 442 FW. Refer
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility (OPR) using the Air Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the Air force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://afrim.amc.af.mil/ .


14.23.6.1. (Added-442FW) The 442d Fighter Wing hot refueling sites on Whiteman AFB are
located on the North Ramp, parking spots 1-18 with coordinates, see attachment #1. Parking plan
details see attachment #3.

14.23.6.2. (Added-442FW) R-11 fuel trucks will be used to perform hot refuels at Whiteman
AFB.

14.23.6.3. (Added-442FW) the 442d Fighter Wing hot refueling sites on Whiteman AFB are in
compliance with the quantity-distance separation requirements as listed in AFMAN 91-201,
Explosives Safety Standards, see attachment #2.
