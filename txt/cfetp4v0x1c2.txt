SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The CFETP is revised to accommodate for the Optometry and Ophthalmology career field merger. This change will
streamline current optometry instructional requirements and add new ophthalmology tasks to the Ophthalmic
Apprentice Course. The items listed above that are changing to a proficiency code “-“ are a duplication of training
and therefore do not need to be designated with their current proficiency code. The items listed above that are
changing to either an “A” or “2b” are being added to the course due to an ophthalmology requirement.
