This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 36-25, Military Promotion and
Demotion; and delegates the AMC Vice Commander (AMC/CV) , Eighteenth Air Force
Commander (18 AF/CC), and Director of Personnel (HQ AMC/A1) to take certain personnel
actions for the commander and to establish procedures for these actions. This publication does
not apply to Air National Guard (ANG) and United States Air Force Reserve (AFR) units.
However, ANG and AFR units are encouraged to utilize the guidance when appropriate. Refer
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route
AF Form 847s from the field through the appropriate functional chain of command. Ensure that
all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of
in accordance with Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located in Records
Information     Management      System     (AFRIMS)      at    Air    Force    (AF)     Portal:
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. This revision
adds MLR President designation as outlined in Air Force Instruction (AFI) 36-2406, Officer and
Enlisted Evaluation Systems, and USAF EC/CC delegation.
 2                                                              AMCI36-2501 26 JULY 2012


1. AMC/CV. CV is authorized to accomplish the review and signature requirements outlined in
AFI 36-3206, Administrative Discharge Procedures for Commissioned Officers, paragraph 4.22.
2. AMC/CV. CV is authorized to act for Commander, AMC, to accomplish the review and
signature requirements of AFI 36-3203, Service Retirements, as outlined in paragraph 7.5.2.1.
3. AMC/CV. CV is authorized to accomplish the review and signature requirements outlined in
AFI 36-3207, Separating Commissioned Officers, Sections 2B and 2C.
4. AMC/CV. CV is designated as the AMC MLR President and promotion authority for
promotion to captain.
5. HQ AMC/A1. A1 is authorized to act for the commander, AMC, regarding senior master
sergeant and chief master sergeant promotions and administrative demotions for those
individuals assigned to AMC directorates, special staff agencies, field operating agencies, and
other direct reporting unit personnel, as outlined in AFI 36-2502.
6. 18 AF/CC. 18 AF/CC is authorized to act for the commander, AMC, regarding senior master
sergeant and chief master sergeant promotions and administrative demotions for those
individuals assigned to numbered air force, 618th Air Operations Center, wings, and stand-alone
groups that fall under 18 AF, as outlined in AFI 36-2502.
7. USAF EC/CC. USAF EC/CC is authorized to act for the commander, AMC, regarding
senior master sergeant and chief master sergeant promotions and administrative demotions for
those individuals assigned to expeditionary center, schools, wings and groups that fall under
USAF EC.
