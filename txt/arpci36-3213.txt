This instruction implements AFI AFI 36-3209, Separation and Retirement Procedures for Air National
Guard and Air Reserve Members. This instruction outlines the preparation and use of ARPC IMT 88,
Election of Options.
This instruction includes a requirement to maintain, collect, use, or disseminate information subject to the
Privacy Act of 1974. AUTHORITY: Title 10 U.S.C., Section 1168.

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS
This revision rewords paragraph 2. for clarification and deletes the choice for members to find an assign-
ment. This revision also changes Form to IMT. A bar ( | ) indicates a revision from the previous edition.


1. Reference. AFI 36-3209.

2. Purpose. ARPC IMT 88 is used as an attachment to the Inactive Status List Reserve Section (ISLRS)
letters to members who have been in ISLRS over 3 years and must voluntary resign their commission or
be involuntary discharged.

3. Procedures.
   3.1. Reserve officers who have been in ISLRS over 3 years will receive a notice of proposed action
   with an attached ARPC IMT 88 to make their election.
   3.2. If the completed form is not received the Reserve officer will receive a discharge order and hon-
   orable discharge certificate in approximately 90 days.
