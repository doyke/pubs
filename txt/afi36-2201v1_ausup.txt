SUMMARY OF REVISIONS

This revision rearranges paragraphs from the previous version of the supplement to align with
the new paragraph arrangement in AFI 36-2201. Some paragraphs have been reworded for
clarity. Due to the dissolution of the Academic Instruction School (AIS) on 1 Oct 2003, all
references to AIS have been removed from the instruction.

AFI 36-2201, Volume 1, 1 October 2002, is supplemented as follows:

1.2.1. (Added) (AU) All Air University (AU) schools or institutions hosting instructional
programs coordinate with Academic Affairs Division (HQ AU/CFA) during development or
review of internal regulations, operating instructions, unit-generated Instructional System
Development (ISD) modifications to the basic USAF ISD model, and manuals that affect the
ISD process as applied at AU.

1.3.5.1. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA serves as office of primary responsibility (OPR) for ISD
matters at AU.

1.3.5.2. (Added) (AU) AU schools/institutions appoint an ISD monitor or focal point to
coordinate on ISD matters with the AU OPR.

1.3.9. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA provides representatives or coordinates on selection of
appropriate representatives for those Air Force, MAJCOM, FOA, or DRU TPTs, U&TWs,
STS/CTS conferences, ISD conferences, etc., that pertain to AU.

1.3.10. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA prepares or coordinates on Air Force-numbered publications
assigned to AU regarding ISD concepts and AU publications that introduce ISD concepts.

1.3.11. (Added) (AU) AU schools develop local procedures to:

  1.3.11.1. (Added) (AU) Provide orientation and training to instructors, faculty supervisors,
and administrators to ensure effective implementation and management of ISD in their school.

  1.3.11.2. (Added) (AU) Ensure to the maximum extent possible the use of ISD by each level
of instructional developers, instructional managers, and instructional supervisors within the
organization.

1.3.12. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA reviews curriculum plans for application of the ISD process
(See AUI 36-2306, Air University Curriculum and Program Review).

1.3.13. (Added) (AU) Commanders and commandants of AU schools identify instructional
program requirements based on sound analysis of the instructional situation as described by the
ISD process.

1.4.2.1. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA ensures functional OPRs and instructional activities at AU
are apprised of current research findings that impact curriculum development and
implementation processes.

1.4.4.1. (Added) (AU) HQ AU/CFA coordinates with AU schools on matters concerning
application of ISD and instructional technology related to nonresident education.
