AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 10, is supplemented as follows: This supplement
implements procedures and guidance in AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chapter 10, Physical
Asset Management. This supplement establishes additional requirements not otherwise specified
in this publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this
publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Refer recommended changes and questions
about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from the field through the
appropriate functional chain of command.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. This publication
consolidates previously supplemented Chapter 10, Receipt Processing Overview; Chapter 14,
Storage and Related Operations; Chapter 20, Inventory Procedures; and Chapter 24, Repair
Cycle Support Element.

10.3.4.1. The Chief Inspector will ensure sections/elements establish a program to thoroughly
inspect all items and duties are adhered to. War Readiness personnel will conduct the functional

covers will be used to maintain the integrity of the barcode label. Hazmart will verify any
barcode inquiries.
10.3.5.2.2. The Compliance and Analysis Section will ensure the Materiel Management Flight

clerks, and warehouse personnel. Only written signatures will be used.

10B1.3.4.5. Option 2, 10B1.3.4.5.2 will be used. If at least two copies are available, leave copy
2 with the property until a notice to stock or a due-out release is received. Use Copy 1 to
annotate in-checker and inspection actions and forward to the terminal for processing. After
initial processing, forward Copy 1 to Document Control. When Copy 1 is marred or hard to
read, the most legible copy will be used as the Document Control copy.

10B4.5.1. (added) Receiving personnel will process Supply Discrepancy Reports (SDR) IAW

processing.

10B5.3.5.1. Receiving personnel will take action to clear 317 rejects.

10B5.3.5.2.3. (added) All Stock Control functions identified in AFMAN 23-110, Vol II, Pt, 2,

Section.
