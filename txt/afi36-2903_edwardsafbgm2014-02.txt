SUBJECT: Edwards AFB Guidance Memorandum, Dress and Personal Appearance of Air
          Force Personnel

RELEASABILITY: There are no releasability restrictions on this publication.

1. By Order of the Edwards Air Force Base Commander, this Guidance Memorandum
immediately authorizes Flight Duty Uniform wear changes in accordance with AFI 36-2903,
Dress and Personal Appearance of Air Force Personnel. Compliance with this Memorandum is
mandatory. This Memorandum supplements AFI 36-2903, Dress and Personal Appearance of
Air Force Personnel by authorizing the wear of morale patches on the Flight Duty Uniform.

2. According to AFI 36-2903, Wing Commanders may authorize the wear of morale patches on
the shoulders of the Flight Duty Uniform on Fridays, or during special events. Small morale tabs
may be worn on the exposed Velcro of the left sleeve when the pen pocket cover is removed;
wear is not restricted.

3. Squadron Commanders will approve and maintain a list of acceptable morale patches and tabs
for wear by assigned Airmen. Morale patches are to remain professional, contain no image or
language that could bring discredit upon the Air Force and the 412th Test Wing.

4. This Memorandum becomes void after one year has elapsed from the date of this
Memorandum, or upon publication of an Interim Change or rewrite of AFI 36-2903, Dress and
Personal Appearance of Air Force Personnel, whichever is earlier.
