AFI25-201_USAFESUP1, Support Agreement Procedures, 7 November 2005 is supplemented as
follows. This supplement applies to all 435th Air Base Wing and tenant units that are serviced by the
435th Logistics Readiness Squadron (LRS). This supplement establishes 435 ABW timelines and
requirements for support agreement completion and review. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN)
33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located in Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS):
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially revised and must be completely reviewed.
(435 ABW) AFI25-201 USAFE Sup 1, 7 Nov 2005, is supplemented as follows: This supplement
provides policy, guidance and procedures to Ramstein AB units involved in creating, reviewing or
terminating installation level support agreements. This supplement applies to all 435 ABW units.
(435 ABW) Identifies timelines for agreement completion and review (paragraphs 2.2.1.5.1. thru
2.2.1.5.6., 2.2.1.10.1., 2.4.1.1.1., 2.5.1.1.1., 2.6.1.1.1., 2.6.1.1.2., 2.6.1.3.1. (added))
2.2.1.5.1. (435 ABW) SAM will set the following suspense to FAACs when coordinating new
agreements.
2.2.1.5.1.1. (435 ABW) Initial FAAC Review will be completed NLT 30 days from initial notification
of support request. SAM will notify FAAC of support request.
2.2.1.5.1.2. (435 ABW) Supplier/Receiver negotiation will be completed NLT 30 Days from completion
of FAAC review.

2.2.1.5.1.3. (435 ABW) Specific provisions impasses will be routed to MAJCOM FAM for resolution as
soon as the impasse is discovered. If specific provision impasse cannot be resolved within 75 days from
initial FAAC notification, the agreement will be routed with impasse per para. 5.5.1.2.
2.2.1.5.1.4. (435 ABW) Final five (CE, SFS, CPTS, MO, JA) reviewers have no more than 10 days each
to review, sign and forward agreement to the next party for review/signature.
2.2.1.5.1.5. (435 ABW) Support agreements will be routed via SharePoint©. If SharePoint© is not
available, agreements will be hand carried through approval process.
2.2.1.5.1.5.1. (435 ABW) SharePoint© support agreement file will use the naming convention below:
FB5612 – XXXX (Folder name = support agreement number)
00 – Staff Summary Sheet
01 – DD Form 1144
01a – Final Five Signature
02 – Specific Provisions
03 – Manpower annex
04 – Funding Annex
05 – Facility Annex
06 – Final Signed
2.2.1.5.1.6. (435 ABW) The SAM will review support agreement status on a weekly basis and notify
respective FAACs or appropriate level commanders of delays.
2.2.1.10.1. (435 ABW) Electronic version of support agreements are                        located   at:
https://ice.usafe.af.mil/sites/435LRG/LRS/LGR/SupportAgreements/default.aspx
2.2.2.1.3. (435 ABW) Post support agreement matrix to the HQ USAFE/A4RI Community of Practice
quarterly.
2.4.1.1.1. (435 ABW) Specific provisions will be reviewed annually by FAACs.
2.5.1.1.1. (435 ABW) The initial manpower annex will be completed NLT 10 days after notification
from SAM.
2.6.1.1.1. (435 ABW) The SAM will provide FM with a list of agreements that are due annual review
NLT 30 Jun.
2.6.1.1.2. (435 ABW) FM will complete annual financial annex review within 90 days of SAM
notification.
2.6.1.3.1. (435 ABW) The initial funding annex will be completed NLT 30 days after notification from
SAM.
