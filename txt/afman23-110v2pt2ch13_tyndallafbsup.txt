This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply System Returns (Turn-Ins) to the
Retail Supply System. It applies to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve, and
Air Force Guard organizations supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in
contract or support agreement terms or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Send
recommended changes and questions about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF IMT
847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. This publication requires collecting and
maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and
E.O. 9397. System of records notice F023 AF IL C applies.

AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 13, is supplemented as follows:

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

13.2.2.1. The Flight Service Center is the designated location to process customer recoverable
item turn-ins. Central Storage After-Hours support may process turn-ins in support of mission
critical serviceable returns, unserviceable two-level maintenance, or AF critical item
evacuations.

13B3.2.2.1. Vehicle Operations will pick up repair cycle items returns from on-base activities.

13B3.2.2.4. Vehicle Operations personnel will sign and date the customer’s AF Form 2520,
Repair Cycle Control Log, or other local product to acknowledge custody transfer of reparable
items.

13B3.2.6. Flight Service Center will process turn-in (TIN) transaction

13B5.1.2. Scrap metal (unserviceable ERRCD XB) from base organization is taken directly to
the 6000 area disposal site. However, unserviceable ERRCD XB materiel containing precious
metals must be transferred to DRMS for reclamation.

13B5.2.1. A scheduled pick-up time is provided for each collection point. Customers may
contact Vehicle Operations Dispatch (283-4872) to add, change, or delete collection point
locations, arrange/change pick-up times, or coordinate pick-up of oversized/bulk items.

13B5.5. Vehicle Operations checks collections points and delivers recovered consumable
property to the Flight Service Center for preparation of turn-in documentation.

13B9.3. Equipment turn-ins will be processed pre-post.

13B9.5. The Customer Service Equipment Management Function will process returns of non-
accountable equipment returns on in-use detail records as pre-post turn-ins.
13.4. Forms (Adopted and Prescribed).
13.4.1. Adopted Forms:
AF Form 2520, Repair Cycle Control Log
13.4.2. Prescribed Forms:
No forms are prescribed by this publication.
