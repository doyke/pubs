This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 36-29, Military Standards, and
establishes the order of precedence for all military personnel at United States Air Force (USAF)
Academy-sponsored events. This instruction applies to all USAF Academy personnel. This
publication does not apply to Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC) units or the Air National
Guard (ANG). Refer recommended changes and conflicts between this and other publications to
HQ USAFA/CCP, 2304 Cadet Drive, Suite 3400, USAF Academy CO 80840, on Air Force (AF)
Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. Ensure that all records created as a
result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force
Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air
Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule
(RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. See Attachment 1
for a glossary of references and supporting information.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This revision updates paragraph 2 by adding the MA to Superintendent, the Chief Diversity
Officer, the 10th Air Base Wing Command Chief, the USAFA Chaplain and the order of
precedence in which they are arranged.
1. Reference. Current USAFA Senior Staff List.
2. Order of Precedence. Use the following precedence at USAF Academy-sponsored events:

3. When to Alter the Order of Precedence:
     3.1. At events sponsored by USAFA/CW, place the Commandant first, followed by the
     Superintendent, and then follow the normal precedence except to place the Vice
     Commandant for Strategy and Vice Commandant for Operations before other colonels.
     3.2. At events sponsored by the Dean of the Faculty, place the Dean first, followed by the
     Superintendent and before the USAFA/CW. Also, place the Vice Dean before other
     colonels.
4. Deviating From the Order of Precedence. You may deviate from the order of precedence
with good reason and using good judgment. Please contact Protocol (HQ USAFA/CCP) if you
have any questions.
