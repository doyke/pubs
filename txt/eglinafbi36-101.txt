This instruction implements AFPD 36-1, General Civilian Personnel Provisions and Authorities,
and applies to all civilian personnel assigned, attached or associated with the Eglin AFB
complex. This instruction requires collecting and maintaining information protected by the
Privacy Act (PA) of 1974 authorized by Title 5 U.S.C., Chapters 53, 55, 81, 10 U.S.C. 8012,
E.O. 9397; system of records notice FO36 SAFAAA. Records from this system of records may
be disclosed for any of the blanket routine uses published by the Air Force. The purpose is to
ensure that departing employees properly turn in all materials and equipment, settle accounts and
provide certification for clearance of administrative and security responsibilities. Disclosure is
voluntary; however, failure to provide the information could result in delayed final clearance
processing. Send recommended changes or comments for this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This publication has been revised to add PA Statement and update the Eglin AFB Form 16.
1. The Eglin AFB Form 16, Civilian Clearance Record, is required for civilians terminating
