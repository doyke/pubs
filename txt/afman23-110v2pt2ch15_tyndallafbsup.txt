AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 15, is supplemented as follows:

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The summary of changes include who will process turn-ins, who delivers property to the proper
shipping point, equipment code change, and who will prepare Electronic Turn-In Documents
(ETID).
15.5.2.1.1.1. For items inducted into AFREP repair, Flight Service Center will process the turn-
in using TEX Code ―1‖ to clear the original base DIFM detail and prevent shipment to DRMO.
15.5.2.1.1.3. The service provider delivers property to the appropriate shipping point or to the
regional Defense Reutilization and Marketing Office (DRMO) IAW statement of work.

15B10.6.2. When P equipment excess is held for future requirements, assign Excess Exception
Code 5 to items in stock or transfer accountability to Allowance Standard 000. All L equipment
has been converted to P equipment with implementation of Item Unique Identifier (IUID)
marking, Jan – Mar 2011.

15C8.3.3. Inspection waiver option is taken. Central Storage personnel are responsible for
validating the condition and documentation of serviceable shipments.

15C22.5.3. See local Operating Instruction for additional processing actions required of service
provider employees when handling classified material.
