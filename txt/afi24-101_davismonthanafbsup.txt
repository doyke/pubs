AIR FORCE INSTRUCTION 24-101, is supplemented as follows. The supplement incorporates
procedures for Leave-In-Conjunction with Official (LICWO) Travel (paragraph 3.3.1); use of
Individual Billed Accounts (IBA), Centrally Billed Accounts (CBA) (paragraph 3.4) and the
process of the CBA reconciliation (paragraph 4.1.3.6). Maintain records created as a result of
published processes IAW AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and dispose of records
IAW the AF Records Disposition Schedule (RDS), available from the Air Force Portal at the AF
Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) link. Contact supporting records
managers as required. Send comments and suggested improvements on AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, through 355 LRS/LGRD, 5165 E Kachina Street,
Davis-Monthan AFB, AZ 85707.
3.3. For leave-in-conjunction with official TDY travel, members must contact Commercial
Travel Office (CTO) for costs of official travel and leave. If deviating from normal routing for
the official travel, member must obtain approval and a signed LICWO travel memo from Traffic
Management Flight allowing the member to self-procure one way or roundtrip tickets.
   3.4.1. (Added) The individual Government Travel Card (GTC) must be used for method of
   payment when arranging official travel through the Defense Travel System (DTS) or CTO.
   Individuals not in possession of an individual GTC must have orders stating they are not in
   possession of a GTC and authorized to use Central Billing Account (CBA).
       4.1.3.6. The CBA reconciliation process will start when Bank of America Bill is received.
       CTO must give TMF invoice report for the month. TMF must review all charges and
       credits, ensuring report for payment is correct. TMF must date stamp Bank of America
       Bill and invoice report. TMF will certify for payment and forward the bill for payment.
