SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Major changes
include procedures on inventorying, receiving, handling, tracking and delivering property within
LRS. Addresses Functional Check Listing procedures.
9.1.1. All processes assigned to Requisitioning, Requirements, or references to Stock Control in
this chapter will be performed by the Customer Support Section, 20 LRS/LGRMC, unless
otherwise supplemented.
9.2.5.1. The customer is responsible for ordering all required items in this paragraph through the
Government-Wide Purchase Card (GPC) program.
9.2.5.1.1. (Added) Local purchase items will not be loaded or processed through the Standard
Base Supply System (SBSS) at this base.
9.2.8. The 735 Supply Chain Operations Group (SCOG) is responsible for the review of the
D18, Priority Monitor Report
9.3.7. The Materiel Management Flight Local Manufacture Monitor (LMM) will assume the
related task of Stock Control for field manufacture items. (See ACCI 23-251_SHAWAFBSUP,
Combat Oriented Supply Organization (COSO) Policy, paragraph 4.7 through 4.7.3.4)
9.4.1.1. General Services Administration (GSA) national stock numbers will not be loaded to the
SBSS item record except for direct aircraft Standard Reporting Designator (SRD) items.
9.7.5. NOTE: The Customer Support Liaison Element, 20 LRS/LGRMCC, will process all
Tracer Action Required (TAR) transactions required in Attachment 9G-10, as required.
