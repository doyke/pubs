AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 18, is supplemented as follows. This publication
does not apply to Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC) or Air National Guard (ANG) units and
members. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of Records, and
disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS)
Records     Disposition    Schedule    (RDS)     located    at    https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-

suggested improvements on AF IMT 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; through
channels to 2 LRS/LGLOC, 845 Logistics Lane, Barksdale AFB, LA 71112.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This supplement is initiated to reflect that the Hand Held Terminal (HHT) will be used in place
of personnel signatures. Additionally, how the Delinquent Document Listing, the Daily
Document Listing and Delinquent Shipment Listing will be worked.
18.8.2. The Hand Held Terminal (HHT) will verify the customer’s Personal Identification
Number (PIN) and authorization to sign for the applicable organization code, with the following
exceptions:
   a. “F” type equipment transactions (i.e., FED, FET, etc.)
   b. “1” type transactions (i.e., 1ET, 1MK, 1NK, etc.)

     c. Forced Due-Out Release not linked to an original Standard Asset Tracking System (SATS)

18.8.3.2. Authorization to receipt for classified property will be entered into the SATS.
18.9.2.6. The HHT will verify the customer’s PIN number and authorization to sign for the

18.10.4. The SATS Identification will be used as the in-checker and inspector’s signature for
organization refusals requiring turn-in action.
18.12.2.2.1. The 2 LRS Commander has tasked Document Control with loading Document

18.12.2.2.1. NOTE: 2 LRS Commander has tasked Document Control to maintain source
documents 5 workdays.
18.15.6.3. The daily Delinquent Document Listing, R59 is available on the Global Logistics
Support Center (GLSC) web page.
18.15.6.4. Delinquent document and SATS meeting will be conducted daily with a
representative from each section present. Document Control will email daily R59 reflecting pre-
delinquent and delinquent documents to all monitors and section chiefs. Some geographically
separated organizations may teleconference with an e-mail memo for record as audit trail and
evidence that they are making their best effort to attend meetings.
18.17.4.1. File the electronic Daily Document Register on the "I" drive.
18.19.2. SATS will be used to receipt for equipment/supplies (non BSS/munitions/classified).
(Ref: SATS Users Manual, Para 5.1.4, pg 5-144)
18.19.6. Authorization to receipt for classified property will be entered into SATS.
18.21.1. NOTE: Customer Service will maintain the Authorization Receipt Listing for classified
property and documentation. Document Control will obtain copy of listing from the squadron
server.
18.21.2.1.2. The Master Name List, L44 posted on the 2 LRS homepage under Customer
Service. The L44 will be used to verify authorization to sign for classified equipment when
SATS is down.
18.21.4. NOTE: Customer Service will maintain/update the Authorization Receipt Listing for
classified property and documentation. Document Control will obtain copy of listing from the
squadron server.
18.24.5. The Delinquent Shipment Listing, R40 is available on the GLSC Web page. Document
Control will provide Part 5 to Storage and Issue, R-40 monitor and Part 6 to Transportation,
Traffic Management office, and Shipment Distribution Center supervisor to be administered in
accordance with the provision outlined in AFI 24-203, Chapter 10.
18.34.6. Either sections delinquents or pre-delinquents on the Delinquent Document Listing
(DDL) will be printed.
