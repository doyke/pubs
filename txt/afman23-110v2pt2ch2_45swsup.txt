This supplement implements and extends guidance of Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, V2,
PT2 and AFMAN 23-110, V2, PT2, AFSPC Sup 1. This supplement describes 45 SW
procedures for use in conjunction with the basic AFMAN and AFSPC Supplement. It applies to
all 45 SW activities, tenants, and contractors operated or supported by the USAF Standard Base
Supply System. This supplement does apply to Air Force Reserve Command and Air National
Guard units.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This 45 SW supplement reflects reference and minor changes in AFMAN 23-110, V2, PT2.

CHAPTER 2

2.3.3. Delegations of signature authority to functional supervisors will be designated in writing
and in the specific paragraph, chapter, and supplement to which they apply. Authority to sign
accountable documents (M10, Reports of Survey, etc.) is the Accountable Officer
45 LRF/LGRM. Accountable documents shall be maintained on file by the Document Control
Element Base Supply for timeframes specified for the governing document.

2.25. The Funds Manager (assigned to 45 LRF/CC), Supply Manager and the Accountable
Officer are designated the supply representative to the financial working group (FWG).

2.27.8.2. Evaluating Incoming Discrepancies. Establish local procedures to prevent following up
on reports when the benefit would be far less than the administrative cost of processing the
reports. EXCEPTION: Always follow up on discrepancy reports involving classified items
regardless of cost. Volume 1, part 1, chapter 5, section D, contains further information on
discrepancy reports.

2.28.4.7. Option 2 in Chapter 23 has been selected. The semiannual analysis of out-processing
procedures is not required.

2.29.4. The Document Control office will maintain the Classified Authorization Receipt Listing
and documentation that authorizes individuals to receipt for classified property.

2.32.12. Customer Service will maintain and manage the SATS authorization and will act as the
System Administrator for Customer authorizations.

2.41.13. The authority to approve or disapprove authorization changes within applicable
allowance document basis of issue is delegated to Equipment Management Element.

2.49.6. Election to work with base contracting to obtain the following for local purchase (JBB)
receipts; missing local purchase status (LPS) and DD Form 1155, Order for Supplies or Services,
request for quotations is selected.

2.55.2.2. The Time Compliance Technical Order (TCTO) and stock lists are located in Customer
Service.

2.60. The 45 LRF/FD/FC has the responsibility for SORTS reporting. The SBSS Contractor
will provide the data in the format requested by the 45 LRF/FD/FC.

2.77.5.4. On a case-by-case basis the election to change the published surveillance schedule for
elements rated during MAJCOM compliance inspections is elected.
