This supplement provides guidance and is applicable to all AFMC units. It expands the guidance
provided in the Air Force Instruction 91-103 to support the reporting requirements of HQ
AFMC. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located in Air Force
Records       Information   Management      System    (AFRIMS)       on    the     AF     Portal:
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm). Refer recommended changes and
questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF
Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from the field
through the appropriate functional chain of command. It incorporates publications formats and
tailors the requirements of AFI 91-103 to AFMC operations.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This revision aligns the AFMC supplement with the Air Force Instruction. Several paragraphs
have been rearranged, and rewritten for clarification and improved readability. It updates the
AFMC organizational office symbol changes. Numerous changes and additions for AFMC
specific nuclear surety program and changes to organizational office symbols mandate a
complete review of this supplement.
3.4.1. (Added) AFMC) HQ AFMC/SEW manages the nuclear certification program for the
command.

3.5.3.6. (Added) AFMC) Nuclear Surety Evaluations (NSEs) are the responsibility of the
developing agency for hardware, software, facilities and procedures. NSEs must be coordinated
through the center/wing weapons safety office and forwarded to HQ AFSC/SEW with a copy to
AFNWC (independent review agency).
3.5.3.7. (Added) AFMC) AFNWC will evaluate support equipment provided by other DoD
agencies for use with Air Force procedures.
3.5.3.8. (Added) AFMC) AFNWC evaluates Air Force use of Department of Energy (DOE)-
certified equipment.
3.5.3.10. (Added) AFMC) AFNWC will assist HQ AFSC/SEW and the developing agency in
resolving any specific nuclear surety concern and ensure corrective action is taken when
required.
7.2. (Added) AFMC) Send NCIS to AFNWC to begin coordination process. A copy will be sent
to HQ AFMC/SEW.
8.1. (Added) AFMC) Send copies of the OPCERT and DECERT procedures to HQ AFSC/SEW
for approval and to AFNWC as an informational copy.
