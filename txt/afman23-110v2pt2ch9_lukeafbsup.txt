9B24.24.2. Request Air Force Repair Enhancement Program (AFREP) personnel to process
issue (ISU) and special requisition (SPR) for Defense Reutilization and Marketing Service
(DRMS) withdrawals using prescribed guidelines.
9B24.24.2.1. (Added) Customer Support will maintain an off-line (manual) requisition number
log to manage withdrawals from DRMS.
9B24.24.2.2. (Added) The AFREP personnel will provide Customer Support with all pertinent
information such as name, organization, office symbol, phone number, intended use, stock
number, nomenclature, and quantity. Customer Support will provide AFREP personnel with a
document number to process an ISU transaction with TEX Code H, and an appropriate demand
code. This transaction will create a memo due-out detail to the customer’s organization.
9B24.24.2.3. (Added) The AFREP personnel will process a SPR transaction with (K) serial
numbers 800 to 825, which will be provided by Customer Support.
