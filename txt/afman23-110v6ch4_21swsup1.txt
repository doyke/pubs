The OPR for this supplement is CORSC/LGRSP (Mr. Paul L. Bentley). This supplement implements
and extends the guidance of Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, Volume 2, Chapter 4, Precious
Metals Recovery Program (PMRP). This supplement describes 21 SW procedures for use in
conjunction with the basic AFMAN. It applies to all Peterson AFB Standard Base Supply System
(SBSS) functions. It also applies to all activities assigned to and those activities supported by the 21st
Space Wing. Maintain and dispose of records created as a result of prescribed processes in accordance
with AFMAN 37-139, Records Disposition Schedule (will convert to AFMAN 33-322, Volume 4).

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS

Paragraphs 4.2.1 and 4.9.1 delete the requirement for a BPMRPM at OLSA. A bar ( | ) indicates a
revision from the previous edition.

4.2.1. The Chief, Inspection Element (PMI/LGRSDI), is appointed as the Base Precious Metals
Recovery Program Manager (BPMRPM).

4.2.6. Activities give the BPMRPM a copy of the letter of appointments.

4.9.1. Supply account 620PM is set up for the BPMRPM. All precious metal turn- ins (residue, etc.),
except that generated by medical activities, must be turned in to the BPMRPM located at Peterson AFB,
Bldg 662. The Peterson AFB BPMRPM transports the property to the Defense Reutilization and
Marketing Office (DRMO), Ft Carson CO.
