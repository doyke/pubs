                                   AFSC 4V0X1
                                  OPHTHALMIC
                    CAREER FIELD EDUCATION AND TRAINING PLAN

Summary: This CFETP removes the certification requirement at the 5-skill level; changes the
wording of the 7-skill level requirements to clarify certification; and removes the requirement for
Air Reserve Component personnel as these duties are not routinely performed. This change is
effective immediately.

1. Pen and ink the following changes:

  Page   Line/Paragraph     Change
  7      1.3.2.2.           Delete “(6) Must receive Ophthalmic certification from a nationally
                            accredited body (i.e. JCAHPO or AOA).” [Reference
                            CFETP4V0X1C1, dated 18 August 2008]
  7      1.3.2.3.           Change to read “(5) Must receive Ophthalmic certification from a
                            nationally accredited body (i.e. JCAHPO or AOA). Exception: Air
                            National Guard and Air Force Reserve personnel are exempt from
                            this requirement.

2. Page Inserts: None.

3. After necessary action, file this sheet behind CFETP, Change 1 and Change 2.
