This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply System Base Civil Engineering
Supply Support Procedures. It applies to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve,
and Air Force Guard organizations supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in
contract or support agreement terms or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/.                Send
recommended changes and questions about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF IMT
847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. This publication requires collecting and
maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and
E.O. 9397. System of records notice F023 AF IL C applies.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The summary of changes include who will perform required research and process new item
record loads, new requirements for SBSS, changes to validation requirements and updates to the
Organization Bench Stock Listing.
31.8. A dedicated warehouse is operated by Base Civil Engineer.
        31.14.2. Customer Service will perform required research and process new item record
        loads.
     31.15. Local Purchase requirements cannot be acquired through the SBSS.
        31.17.1. The R31, Due-Out Status List, is used in lieu of the M09 (BCE Due-Out Status
        listing) to review and validate requirements. Due-out cancellation or update can be
        submitted to Customer Service by any method available; e.g., phone, e-mail, letter, walk-
        in.
31A1.4.7. Use of TEX Code 3 must be authorized by the applicable financial management group.

31A1.4.17. D043 is used as the source of reference for assigning the appropriate nomenclature.

31C1.4, Note 7. The Bench Stock Manager forwards the Organization Bench Stock Listing in
item number sequence in Excel format via email to bench stock custodians. Custodians can sort
the Excel spreadsheet in any format desired to include NSN sequence. MO4 changes are based
on 60-day bench stock quantities.
