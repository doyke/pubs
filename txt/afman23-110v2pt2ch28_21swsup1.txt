The OPR for this supplement is CORSC/LGRSP (Mr. Paul L. Bentley). This supplement implements and
extends the guidance of Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 5, Satellite
Procedures. This supplement describes 21 SW procedures for use in conjunction with the basic AFMAN. It
applies to all Peterson AFB Standard Base Supply System (SBSS) functions. It also applies to all activities
assigned to and those activities supported by the 21 SW. Maintain and dispose of records created as a result
of prescribed processes in accordance with AFMAN 37-139, Records Disposition Schedule (will convert to
AFMAN 33-322, Volume 4).

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS

Deletes paragraphs 28.4.5, 28.5.1.1, 28.8.1.1, 28.8.1.2, 28.8.1.3, 28.8.2, 28.8.3, 28.11.1, 28.13.1, 28.14.1,
28.17.2, 28.17.2.1, 28.17.2.3, 28.18.2. Paragraphs 28.4.7, 28.5.2, 28.6.1, 28.7.1, 28.7.5.2, 28.8.1, 28.9.1,
28.10.1, 28.16.1, 28.20.1 and 28.20.2 delete reference to OLBU, OLCM, and OLFA and add system
designator A8. A bar ( | ) indicates a revision from the previous edition.

28.4.7. Base Contracting capability does not exist at Thule AB for system designator A8.

28.5.2. All local L and P serialized number loads for system designator A8 are accomplished by CSB
Customer Support.

28.6.1. The CSB performs all stock control functions for system designator A8.

28.7.1. All requisitioning action for system designator A8 is done by the CSB Stock Control.

28.7.5.2. All off-line follow-ups for system designator A8 are done by CSB Stock Control.

28.8.1. Issue requests for system designator A8 are processed by CSB Customer Service.

28.9.1. CSB Material Handling processes all receipts for system designator A8.

28.10.1. System designator A8 customers request cancellation through CSB Stock Control either in writing or
by telephone.

28.16.1. The CSB performs all document control functions. Thule AB supply personnel send all System
designator A8 documents to the CSB as soon as possible using the distribution system.

28.20.1. The CSB accomplishes full equipment support for system designator A8 customers.

28.20.2. The Thule supply supervisor for system designator A8 designates at least one individual and an
alternate, knowledgeable in equipment supply processing, to advise appropriate custodians on equipment
document processing.
