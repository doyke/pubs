                   PROCEDURES FOR BLDG
                         When Warned or Directed to Take Immediate Shelter:
                      Ensure other facility occupants are informed to take shelter.
                      If in a modular/non-permanent building, and time permits, relocate to a
                      more suitable structure or permanent facility -- use good judgment!
                      Report to the designated Natural Disaster shelter location IAW the
                      Facility Sheltering Plan -- follow established procedures.
                      If shelter location is unknown; get to the lowest floor level, innermost
                      and center of room away from windows, exterior facility walls or doors.
     Natural Disaster Remain sheltered until the “All Clear” announcement is given.

                      Report personnel status to UCC, CSS or 442 FW Command Post.
                         When Warned or Directed to Shelter-In-Place due to
                         accidental/intentional Hazardous Materials or CBRN Release:
                         Ensure other facility occupants are informed to take shelter.
                         Report to the designated Hazardous Materials (HazMat)/CBRN Release
                         SIP location IAW the Facility Sheltering Plan -- follow established
                         procedures and Facility Manager, SIP Manager or senior member
                         instructions. Use SIP kit supplies to seal off room from vapors.
                         If SIP location is unknown; get to the highest floor level possible,
                         innermost room with the fewest windows and doors. Shutdown heating,
      HazMat/CBRN
