This instruction implements AFPD 34-12, Air Force Protocol, AFI 34-1201, Protocol and Air
Force Pamphlet (AFPAM) 34-1202, Guide to Protocol. This instruction applies to all
Department of Defense organizations and members who wish to reserve and check out
ceremonial items for events and ceremonies from the 21st Space Wing Protocol Office. This
publication applies to members of the Air Force Reserve and Air National Guard. Refer
recommended changes and conflicts between this and other publications to office of primary
responsibility, on Air Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication.
Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance with AFMAN33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims. This publication may not be supplemented.

1. Purpose. 21 SW Form 12, Ceremonial Item Request Form, establishes an electronic means
to allow customers to request ceremonial items for their events and ceremonies. This form will
create a more efficient means for action officers to conduct their ceremonies by reserving the
required items to execute their ceremonies and for 21st Space Wing Protocol to track their
ceremonial item inventory. The form will be electronically submitted to the 21st Space Wing
Protocol office to reserve the items.
2. Flag Protocol. Members will locate the directions/sample for the 21 SW Form 12, on the
21st Space Wing Protocol Share Point site and submit. Due to limited inventory, it is important

to submit the request for ceremonial items as soon as possible, to ensure the items are available
for the event or ceremony.
3. References. See AFP 34-1202, Guide to Protocol, Chapter 14 for protocol guidance
concerning custom and procedures for military ceremonies including funerals, retirements,
promotions, changes and assumptions of command, activations, inactivations, re-designations,
reenlistments, awards, decorations, reveille, retreat, building rededications, ribbon cutting and
POW/MIA.
