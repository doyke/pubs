Air Force Instruction (AFI 34-219), 17 October 2007, is supplemented as follows: This supplement
applies to all units assigned to Holloman AFB, NM. Commanders will give this supplement wide
dissemination to apprise personnel under their jurisdiction of the procedures contained herein, the
necessity of compliance and the consequence of noncompliance. Ensure that all records created as a
result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with (IAW) Air Force
Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force
Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact supporting records managers as
required. Refer recommended changes and questions to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR)
using AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication, to 49th Force Support Squadron
Sustainment Services Flight Clubs (49 FSS/FSVC), 280 First Street, Holloman AFB, NM 88330-8273.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This change implements new guidance that clarifies the authorized locations for on-premise
consumption as well as the authority for dispensing alcohol. A margin bar ( | ) indicates newly
revised material.

1.3.1.1. (Added) In accordance with New Mexico State law, military members, dependents,
and other civilians must be at least 21 years of age to purchase, possess, be served, or consume
alcoholic beverages. Under no circumstances will alcoholic beverages be served to underage
individuals.
           1.3.2.1. (Added) IAW New Mexico State law, 19 years of age is the minimum age
           for someone to serve alcohol. A 19-year-old may serve alcohol under restaurant
           conditions, after verifying the age of the patron. A 19-year-old may not serve alcohol
           in the bar or be employed as a bartender.
       1.5.4. (Added) Persons creating an unfavorable incident at any Force Support or Army
       and Air Force Exchange Service (AAFES) facility as a result of overindulgence in
       alcoholic beverages will have their patronage privileges suspended for one year.
       Requests for reinstatement of privileges will be made in writing to the 49th Mission
       Support Group Commander (49 MSG/CC).
           2.1.6.1. (Added) The 49th Force Support Squadron (49 FSS) and AAFES are the
           only authorized agents to sell and dispense alcoholic beverages on Holloman AFB.
           2.1.6.2. (Added) Holloman's Clubs, Golf Course, Bowling Center, Base Theater,
           and Lodging may sell alcoholic beverages by the drink, for on-premise consumption,
           during their individual approved hours of operation.
           2.1.6.3. (Added) The 49 FSS will be the only source of selling and dispensing
           alcoholic beverages for on-premise consumption during special events in, or catered
           by, a 49 FSS facility such as wedding parties, unit functions, private parties, etc.
           When possible, underage patrons will not be permitted in the same area where alcohol
           is being served.
           2.1.6.4. (Added) Steinhoff and Heritage parks, 49 FSS athletic fields, FAMCAMP,
           equestrian facility, base housing, dormitories, dormitory picnic areas, unit picnic
           areas, Lodging, lodging picnic areas, and other areas approved in writing by the 49
           MSG/CC are authorized areas for the consumption of alcoholic beverages by persons
           of legal age. Alcoholic beverages are prohibited at the 49 FSS Apache Sports Range.
           2.1.6.5. (Added) Alcoholic beverages may be consumed at base special events such
           as Independence Day, Oktoberfest, Open Houses, concerts, etc. with 49 MSG/CC
           approval.
