Air Force Instruction (AFI) 36-2905, Fitness Program, 1 July 2010, to include Air Force
Guidance Memorandum 3 (AFGM3), 3 January 2012, is supplemented as follows: The
purpose of this supplement is to establish policy and procedures unique for Incirlik Air Base
(AB), Turkey. This supplement applies to all personnel assigned to the 39th Air Base Wing (39
ABW) and transient personnel accomplishing fitness assessments. Refer recommended changes
and questions regarding this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR), 39
FSS/CC using AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. Ensure that all
records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of
in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/.

1.21.4.3. (Added) FAC Trained Physical Training Leaders (PTLs). Fitness Assessment
Cell (FAC) PTL Augmentee trained personnel are required to administer all portions of the
fitness assessment in accordance with (IAW) AFI 36-2905, Chapter 2. To ensure mission
completion, unit PTLs will remain current on testing procedures and units will provide PTL FAC
augmentation in the following order:
1.21.4.3.1. (Added) January – 39th Logistics Readiness Squadron (39 LRS) male and
39th Comptroller Squadron (39 CPTS) female.
1.21.4.3.2. (Added) February – 39th Communications Squadron (39 CS) male and 39th Civil
Engineering Squadron (39 CES) female.

1.21.4.3.3. (Added) March      – 39th Security Forces Squadron (39 SFS) male and
39th Maintenance Squadron (39 MXS) female.
1.21.4.3.4. (Added) April – 39 CES male and 39th Medical Operations Squadron (39 MDOS)
female.
1.21.4.3.5. (Added) May – 39th Medical Support Squadron (39 MDSS) male and 39th Force

1.21.4.3.6. (Added) June – 39 MXS male and 39 LRS female.
1.21.4.3.7. (Added) July – 39 CPTS male and 728th Air Mobility Squadron (728 AMS)

1.21.4.3.8. (Added) August – 39th Operations Squadron (39 OS) male and 39 SFS female.
1.21.4.3.9. (Added) September - 39 FSS male and 39 ABW Staff Agencies/Armed Forces
Network (AFN) female.
1.21.4.3.10. (Added) October – 39 MDOS male and 39 CS female.
1.21.4.3.11. (Added) November – 39 ABW Staff Agencies/AFN male and 39 OS female.
1.21.4.3.12. (Added) December – 728 AMS male and 39 MDSS female.

Note: In the event assigned units are unable to provide required FAC trained PTL augmentees
on assigned dates, they remain responsible for coordinating a trained backfill in advance from
other units to ensure coverage.

2.7.1.1. (Added) Airmen testing with the 1.5 mile run as their cardio assessment will normally
be tested in the following order: Abdominal circumference measurement, push ups, sit ups and
then run. This sequence for strength and the run may be changed by FAC Manager if necessary.
2.7.4. (Added) All members tested on the cardio portion will wear the numbered, light-weight
jerseys provided by the FAC.
