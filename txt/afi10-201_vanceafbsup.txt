SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Revised to correspond with revised directive.
1.11.10.2.11. The wing SORTS manager and unit monitors will review the worksheets together
ensuring the worksheets are accurate and complete. Any minor errors or discrepancies will be
corrected at that time by the unit monitor, major errors or discrepancies (i.e., affecting C-levels,
adding or deleting remarks, etc.) will be corrected by the unit monitor and will be briefed to and
re-signed by the releasing official.
1.11.10.2.12. Once unit worksheets are verified for accuracy, a copy will be made for the unit
monitor, as required, and originals will be posted in unit monitor’s classified continuity binder.
 2                                       AFI10-201_VANCEAFBSUP 9 DECEMBER 2011


1.11.11.2.4.1. The Wing SORTS manager will send out a quarterly calendar that will list the
date for SORTS turn-in, 71 FTW/CC brief, and report submission.
1.11.10.2.6.4. Invite the point of contact from Manpower and Personnel to attend the annual
training meeting. They will provide recurring training at the annual formal training meeting to
cover current trends or other topics.
1.11.11.1.8. Maintain original unit Designed Operational Capability (DOC) statements and
ensure all required coordination is annotated within 7 days. If unable to coordinate within the
allowed time, ensure a memorandum for record with explanation is posted with the DOC
statement.
2.1. When units are below C-1 on personnel worksheet, Manpower and Personnel Offices must
sign monthly personnel worksheets.
2.4. A unit SORTS monitor must report any C-level change to the Wing SORTS manager within
12 hours after a reportable event or upon direction of the CJCS, the Services or CRO.
