This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive 33-1, Information Resource Management
by establishing a procedure for submitting a work request. This is applicable to all units who are
serviced by 88TH Communication Group, Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio. It does not apply to Air
National Guard or Air Force Reserve Command units. Send comments and suggestions about
this publication for improvements on AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication,
to the Office of Primary Responsibility. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN33-363, Management
of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule
(RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

1. This instruction provides guidance for the WPAFB Form 3215. This form needs to be
completed in its entirety before submitting.
2. This form is used as a formal work request from a customer to the project management
office (88 CG/SCXPR). This form will be used in lieu of AF Form 3215.
