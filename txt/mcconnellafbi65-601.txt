This instruction prescribes the procedures governing the use of private conveyance at government
expense, and obtaining reimbursement for transaction expenses incurred by military/civilian members in
the vicinity of McConnell Air Force Base, Kansas, when conducting official business. This publication
applies to all assigned/tenant organizations.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Major changes include:
Deletes AFR 177-103 as a reference and changes area for the purposes of this instruction;


1. Approval. Submit claims for vicinity travel by the claimant on SF 1164, Claim for Reimbursement
for Expenditures on Official Business. The installation commander or his designated representatives
approves as advantageous to the government.

2. Area. For the purpose of this instruction, McConnell AFB KS vicinity will extend North to Highway
56, East to Highway 99, South Oklahoma State highway 60, and West to U.S. Highway 281.

3. Entitlement. Reimbursement of expenses incurred is at the current The Joint Federal Travel Regula-
tions (JFTR) rate for military members, and the JTR rate for DoD civilian employees. For travel by pri-
vately owned conveyance, mileage is based on odometer readings or other evidence furnished by the
traveler involved.
