This supplement implements and extends the guidance Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110,
USAF Supply Manual, 1 January 2009. This supplement establishes Lajes Field policy and
procedures for the turn-in procedures. These procedures apply to personnel assigned to the
Materiel Management, Systems, and Vehicle Management Flights. Refer recommended changes
and questions about this publication to the OPR using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for
Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from the field to the publication/form manager.
Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with AFMAN 33-363-Management of Records and disposed of in accordance with
the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61a/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.
