            FRAUD,WASTE, AND ABUSE (FWA) PROGRAM

                 FRAUD:
                 • Falsifying travel and other documents or records
                 • Substituting inferior materials
                 • The offer, payment or acceptance of bribes
                 • Conspiring to commit fraud
                 • Conflict of interest relating to procurement or disposal



                                   WASTE:
                                   • Extravagant, careless, or needless expenditure of AF funds
                                   • NOTE: Legitimate stockpiles of arms and ammunition for
                                     wartime needs, though appearing to be redundant and
                                     costly, are not considered waste


                                                          ABUSE:
                                                          • Intentional wrongful or improper use of AF resources
                                                          • Misuse of rank, position or authority that causes the loss
                                                            or misuse of resources
