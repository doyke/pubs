AFI 32-6001, 21 August 2006, and USAFE Supplement, 30 June 2010, are supplemented as
follows:

This supplement sets out policies and procedures relating to 48th Fighter Wing Commander (48
FW/CC) responsibilities for the overall management of Military Family Housing (MFH) for the
RAF Lakenheath and RAF Mildenhall communities. All moves affecting either those on the
Mildenhall Key and Mission Essential (K & E) list or the Mildenhall Officers’ Military Quarters
(OMQ) will be coordinated with the 100th Air Refueling Wing Commander (100 ARW/CC) or
his representative. The duties and responsibilities of the Installation Commander, as outlined in
AFI 32-6001, are delegated to the 48th Mission Support Group Commander. For housing units
on RAF Mildenhall, Installation Commander duties and responsibilities are delegated to the
100th Mission Support Group Commander. This supplement applies to all personnel served by
the 48 FW East Anglia Regional Housing Office. Refer recommended changes and questions
about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, prescribed by Air Force Instruction (AFI) 11-215,
USAF Flight Manuals Program; route AF Form 847s from the field through appropriate
functional’s chain of command. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with (IAW) Air Force Manual
(AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of IAW Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at:
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/.

7.2.3.1. (Added) To ensure economy rentals meet life and safety standards directed by the
Department of Defense (DoD), landlords and rental agents must self-certify a life & safety
checklist provided by the Housing Management Referral Office. Housing referral inspectors will
verify, randomly inspect, and approve all life and safety self-certified checklists, before a lease is
signed with a landlord or rental agent and Overseas Housing Allowance (OHA) is authorized.
Do not sign a lease until you have confirmed with the Housing Management Office that the
property has been inspected and approved for occupancy.
