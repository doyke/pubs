This publication outlines instructions for completing Vance Air Force Base (AFB) Form 5,
Civilian Employee Clearance Record. It applies to all civilian appropriated fund employees
assigned to Vance AFB. This instruction requires collecting and maintaining information
protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by Title 10, United States Code (U.S.C.) 8013,
Secretary of the Air Force; Air Force Instruction 36-2608, Military Personnel Records System;
and Executive Order (E.O.) 9397 (SSN). System of records notice F036 AF PC N, Unit
Assigned Personnel Information, applies. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in the publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN)
33-363, Management of Records, and are disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS)
located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.           Refer recommended
changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR)
using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847 from
the field through the VAFB Publications and Forms Manager. (AF Form 847 is prescribed in
AFI 11-215, USAF Flight Manuals Program [FMP]. Refer to that publication for guidance on
filling out the form.)

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This revision change references from ―VAFB IMT 5‖ to ―VAFB Form 5‖ throughout the
publication. Paragraph 5 was added adopting AF Form 847 Recommendation for Change
Publication.

1. Policy: Separating employees terminating employment from Vance Air Force Base must
clear through the Civilian Personnel Office (71 FSS/FSMC) for clearance procedures and
information on benefits, entitlements, and responsibilities appropriate to the type of separation.
2. Description: VAFB Form 5, Civilian Employee Clearance Record, is printed on white bond
paper, 8 1/2‖ x 11‖. Employees use this clearance record to provide a forwarding address in
order for them to continue to receive any future pay related documents or other personnel items.
3. Procedures: VAFB Form 5 is given to all civilian appropriated fund employees assigned to
Vance Air Force Base by the Civilian Personnel Office. All separating employees must clear all
organizations listed on the VAFB Form 5.
4. Forms Prescribed: VAFB Form 5, Civilian Employee Clearance Record.
5. Forms Adopted: AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change Publication.
