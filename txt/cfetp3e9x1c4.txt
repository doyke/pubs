                                     Summary of Changes

The CFETP is revised to clarify Part I and Part II, Section A, Specialty Training
Standard. This change incorporates changes identified during the Emergency
Management (EM) Panel meeting held at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri 1-5 August 2011
and update FEMA course numbers. Part I paragraph 14.3.1.3. and 14.3.3. require
updating to remove the CDC reference. Part II changes follow: Line items 15.2.1.1.3.,
15.2.1.2.3., 15.2.1.3.3., and 15.2.1.4.3. require changes to the proficiency level in the 7-
level course column (4C(3)). This is redundant training; all EM personnel are required to
be certified yearly in the required suites. Line items 5.9., 10.1.1., 10.2.1., 10.3.1., 10.4.1.,
10.4.2., 10.4.3., 10.4.4., 10.4.4.1., 10.5.1., 10.5.2., 10.6.1., 12.2.1., 12.2.2., 12.2.3.,
