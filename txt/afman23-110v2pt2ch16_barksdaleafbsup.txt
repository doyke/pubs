SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This supplement is initiated to add who is allowed to process reverse post (RVP) and what is
required of the person initiating a reverse post. Additionally, how a RVP log will be maintained.
16.2.2. Document Control, Flight Service Center (FSC), Aircraft Parts Store (APS) and other
authorized personnel (as designated in writing by the 2d Logistics Readiness Squadron
Commander/Chief of Supply (2 LRS/CC/COS) will maintain a log to control RVP actions, IAW
Attachment 16A-3.
16.2.3. (Added) Maintaining RVP Control Log. At the end of each month, FSC, APS and other
authorized personnel will forward a copy of their RVP log to Document Control no later than the
5th workday of the following month. Document Control will then compare the log totals and

Barksdale (BARK) Forms 170, Reverse Post Request to the Monthly Base Supply Management
Report (M32/NGV808) for accuracy. The logs will be kept for 1 year and disposed of IAW
AFRIMS Table & Rule: T 23 - 03 R 17.00
16.4.3. The initiator will prepare BARK Form 170 in three copies for each transaction. Element
supervisors will review BARK Form 170 for accuracy and forward to Flight Commander/Flight
Chief for signature. The freeze code load notice and source document(s) will be hand carried to
Document Control. Once the RVP is completed, Document Control will return one copy of
BARK Form 170 and two copies of the RVP output to the initiator. Document Control will
maintain a separate suspense file for all RVP that requires reprocessing action of the source
documents and coordinate with the responsible area to ensure reprocessing occurs; the suspense
file will be destroyed.
16.4.3.1. (Added) The FSC/APS will use the same procedures listed in paragraph 16.4.3, this
Supplement. The Superintendent of FSC or APS will sign the RVP output source document and
annotate a clear and complete reason for the RVP on the face of the document. The FSC/APS
will forward a copy of the BARK Form 170 along with the source document to Document
Control.
16.4.4. The Zero Overpricing Program monitor will initiate and process RVP inputs when items
are verified as overpriced and forward any accountable documents to Document Control.
Attachment 16A, paragraph 16A3.1A (Added). Write accurate and complete reasons for RVP
actions on the log.
