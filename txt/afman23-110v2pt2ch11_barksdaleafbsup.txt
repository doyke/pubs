SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This supplement is initiated to add additional requirements on how issue request are ordered and
what locally assigned serial numbers and issue exception codes are used.
11.2.2.1. Customer’s issue requests for expendable items will be prepared on AF Form 2005,
Issue/Turn-in Request document.
11.2.2.2. Customer’s request for nonexpendable items will be submitted on AF Form 2005, or
AF Form 601, Equipment Action Request.
11.3.9. Customer’s submit requests to modify non-Mission Capability (MICAP) backorders to
the Stock Control Section.
