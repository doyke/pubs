RELEASABILITY: There are no releasability restrictions on this publication.
       By Order of the Commander, AFISRA, this is a Guidance Memorandum immediately
implementing policy/guidance for the management of AFISRA civilian employee lunch policy to
this Headquarters, subordinate units, and AFISRA-gained Air National Guard and Air Force
Reserve units/personnel. Compliance with this Memorandum is mandatory. To the extent its
directions are inconsistent with other AFISRA publications, the information herein prevails, in
accordance with AFI 33-360, Publications and Forms Management. Requests for waivers must
be submitted through chain of command to the appropriate Tier waiver approval authority, or
alternately, to the Publication OPR for non-tiered compliance items. Maintain records created as
a result of the processes prescribed in this publication in accordance with (IAW) AFMAN 33-
363, Management of Records, and disposed of IAW the Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact your supporting Records
Manager as required.

       The policy in this AFISRA Guidance Memorandum becomes void after one year has
elapsed from the date of this Memorandum, or upon incorporation into a permanent publication,
whichever is earlier.

                                     Guidance Changes

The below change supplements AFI 36-807 dated 21 June 1999 and is effective
immediately.

4.4. All civilian employees will take an unpaid lunch/meal break for a minimum of 30 minutes
and a maximum of 90 minutes. Lunch periods may not be scheduled for the first hour or the last
hour of the workday.
