This supplement implements and extends the guidance in AFI 36-2835, Annual Acquisition Awards
Programs, 16 July 2004. It does not apply to Air Force Reserve Command or Air National Guard units.
Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and are disposed of in accordance with the
Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61/afrims/afrims.
SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This document has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. It includes contracting
awards eligibility changes, and updates nominations submission guidance.
3.1. Nomination Responsibilities and Procedures. All AETC contracting units are eligible and
encouraged to nominate their units and individuals for awards under the contracting awards program.
3.3.1. Units will e-mail nomination packages in time to meet the HQ AETC/A7K suspense date. Include
a transmittal memorandum signed by the wing commander with nomination packages.
3.4. Evaluation Criteria and Panel. HQ AETC/A7K will designate a panel to select no more than one
nominee per award category. An award will not be presented if the selection panel decides nominees’
accomplishments do not justify an award. The selection panel's decision is subject to HQ AETC/A4/7
approval.
3.5. Award Presentation. AETC awards are mailed to individual wing commanders for base-level
presentation.
4.1. Awards and Eligibility. All AETC contracting units are eligible and encouraged to nominate their
units and individuals for awards under the value engineering program.

time to meet the HQ AETC/A7K suspense date. Include a transmittal memorandum signed by the wing
commander with nomination packages.
