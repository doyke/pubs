AFI 90-401, Air Force Relations with Congress, 30 June 2005, is supplemented as follows:
This supplement applies to all units assigned or attached to Hurlburt Field FL, except HQ
AFSOC. This supplement does not apply to the Air Force Reserve (AFRC) and the Air National
Guard (ANG) units. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this
publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Refer recommended changes and questions
about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, route AF Form 847s from the field through the
appropriate functional’s chain of command. See Attachment 1 for a glossary of references and
supporting information.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

This document is substantially revised and must be completely reviewed.
1.8. (Added) Terminology.
1.8.1. (Added) Congressional/High Level Inquiry. Any oral or written inquiry, regardless of the
source, which is intended to provide information to a Member of Congress or their staff, the
Executive Office of the President and Vice President, or other departments of the Executive
Branch of the federal government.

5. (Added) 1 SOW Responsibilities are:
5.1. (Added) 1 SOW/IGQ is the focal point for:
5.1.1. (Added) Ensuring proper and timely command responses to all congressional and high
level inquiries, as set forth in AFPD 90-4, Relations with Congress.
5.1.2. (Added) Identifying the office of primary responsibility (OPR) and/or offices of collateral
responsibility (OCR), if appropriate, for research, analysis and preparation of the 1 SOW/CC
response to congressional or high level inquiries.
5.1.3. (Added) Maintaining the central repository of all congressional and high level inquiries
covered by this supplement.
5.1.4. (Added) Maintaining liaison with HQ AFSOC/IGQ on all congressional and high level
inquiries covered by this supplement.
5.1.5. (Added) When the 1 SOW/IGQ receives a congressional inquiry, IGQ will provide HQ
AFSOC/IGQ with a copy of the congressional and/or high level inquiry. When the 1 SOW/CC
has signed the response, 1 SOW/IGQ will provide HQ AFSOC/IGQ a copy of the response.
5.1.6. (Added) If an IG Investigation is requested by the Congressional Office, the 1 SOW/IGQ
will notify SAF/IGQ through HQ AFSOC/IGQ who will, in turn, notify SAF/LLI, within 24
hours of receipt of congressional inquiry.
5.1.7. (Added) Investigate matters of congressional or high level interest when tasked to do so
by the 1 SOW/IG. Inquiries entered into IG complaint channels are processed according to AFI
90-301, Inspector General Complaints Resolution, and are exempt from the requirements of this
supplement.
5.1.8. (Added) After the 1 SOW/CC has signed the response to the congressional inquiry, 1
SOW/IGQ will dispatch the response to the appropriate address.
5.2. (Added) 1 SOW military and civilian personnel will:
5.2.1. (Added) Immediately inform 1 SOW/IGQ of all congressional and high level inquiries.
Immediately call 1 SOW/IGQ on the phone (884-5567, 884-5534, 884-4439) and then fax (884-
4259) or hand-carry a copy of the written inquiry to 1 SOW/IGQ the same day you receive it.
Transcribe a telephone inquiry and follow the same procedures as you would for a written
inquiry. Never place a congressional or high level inquiry in routine distribution channels.
5.2.2. (Added) When assigned as OPR for preparing a 1 SOW/CC response to congressional or
high level inquiries:
5.2.2.1. (Added) The OPR has 3 duty days to research, analyze and then prepare the response
for the 1 SOW/CC. Example: If the OPR is appointed on Monday, then Tuesday, Wednesday
and Thursday, are the days allotted to research, analyze and prepare the response to include
coordination with 1 SOW/JA, 1 SOW/IG, 1 SOW/CV and signature by 1 SOW/CC. Use
currently accepted software and forward a diskette copy of the response along with the paper
copy to those annotated on the AF Form 1768, Staff Summary Sheet.
5.2.2.2. (Added) The OPR will coordinate the response using AF Form 1768. At a minimum,
responses must be coordinated by the Group Commander responsible for the personnel, program,
policy, or operation addressed in the inquiry, 1 SOW/JA, 1 SOW/IG, and 1 SOW/CV prior to
AFI90-401_HURLBURTFIELDSUP 22 MARCH 2011                                                      3


submission to 1 SOW/CC for signature. See sample at Attachment 2. The OPR will Hand-
carry between offices. Never place a congressional or high level inquiry in routine distribution
channels.
5.2.3. (Added) Expedite the processing of 1 SOW/CC proposed responses to congressional or
high level inquiries.
6. (Added) Associate unit responsibilities for coordinating congressional and high level
inquiries with the 1 SOW are:
6.1. (Added) 1 SOW Inquiries . Immediately inform 1 SOW/IGQ of inquiries involving 1
SOW personnel, programs, policies, or operations. Immediately call 1 SOW/IGQ (884-5567,
884-5534, 884-4439) on the phone and then fax (884-4259) or hand-carry a copy of a written
inquiry to 1 SOW/IGQ the same day that you receive it. Transcribe a telephone inquiry and
follow the same procedures as you would for a written inquiry. Never place a congressional or
high level inquiry in routine distribution channels.
6.1.1. (Added) At a minimum, coordinate the proposed response with 1 SOW/JA, 1 SOW/IG, 1
SOW/CV, and 1 SOW/CC.
6.1.2. (Added) Provide a copy of the response to 1 SOW/IGQ.
