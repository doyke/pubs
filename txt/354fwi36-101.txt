This document applies only to Eielson Air Force Base civilian employees who are serviced by the Eielson
AFB Civilian Personnel Flight.

1. Eielson Air Force Base civilian employees in the bargaining unit represented by the American Federa-
tion of Government Employees Local 1836, who are serviced by the Eielson AFB Civilian Personnel
Flight (CPF), who reside thirteen or more miles south of Fairbanks, Alaska, along the Richardson High-
way, and who have limited base exchange privileges will be issued a Letter of Authorization for Limited
Base Exchange Privileges. This letter will permit bearers limited use of Base Exchange (BX) facilities, in
accordance with 86 FLRR 1-1806, when presented with the Common Access Card (CAC) prior to making
purchases.

2. Employees will relinquish their Letter of Authorization for Limited Base Exchange Privileges to the
CPF when they are no longer a member of the bargaining unit or become otherwise ineligible for limited
BX privileges based upon their location of residence, or when they resign, reassign, retire, or otherwise
separate from employment at Eielson Air Force Base.

3. Letters of Authorization for Limited Base Exchange Privileges may also be issued to Eielson Air Force
Base civilian employees who are serviced by the Eielson Air Force Base CPF, and who are not otherwise
authorized BX privileges by 86 FLRR 1-1806, when the employee has obtained a waiver for use of BX
facilities in accordance with DoDI 1330.21, Armed Services Exchange Regulations (ASER), paragraph
5.5.4.
