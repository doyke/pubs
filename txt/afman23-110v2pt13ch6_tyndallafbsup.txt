This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply Customer's Procedures Repair Cycle
Support. It applies to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve, and Air Force Guard
organizations supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in contract or support
agreement terms or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Send recommended changes and
questions about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF IMT 847, Recommendation for
Change of Publication. This publication requires collecting and maintaining information
protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and E.O. 9397. System of
records notice F023 AF IL C applies.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 13, Chapter 6, is supplemented as follows:

The summary of changes include when TRN transactions are processed, who the point of contact
is for Discrepancy Report Exhibits, CAMS processing and where to find base assembled TCTO
kits.
