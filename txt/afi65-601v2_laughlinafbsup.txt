AFI 65-601, Volume 2, 21 October 1994, is supplemented as follows:

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS: This revision updates format, corrects and add members. A star () indicates new
material supplemented from the basic Air Force instruction.

This supplement sets up the membership and meetings of the Financial Working Group (FWG) and the Financial
Management Board (FMB) for Laughlin AFB.

 10.3.1.1. As a minimum, the FMB membership consists of:

        Wing Commander – Chairperson
        Vice Wing Commander – Member
        Operations Group Commander – Member
        Mission Support Group Commander – Member
        Medical Group Commander – Member
        Director of Maintenance – Member
        Base Civil Engineer – Advisor
        Operational Contracting Officer – Advisor
        Communications Squadron Commander – Advisor
        Civilian Personnel Officer – Advisor
        Financial Management and Comptroller – Advisor
        Financial Analysis Officer – Recorder

10.3.1.2. The FMB meets at least once each quarter or at the request of the chairperson. Financial Analysis Branch
maintains minutes for review by higher authorities and provides a copy to each FMB and FWG member.

10.3.2.1. As a Minimum, the FWG membership consists of:

        Financial Analysis Officer – Chairperson
        Wing Command Section Resource Advisor (RA) – Member
        Maintenance Directorate RA – Member
        Operations Group RA – Member
        Medical Group RA – Member
