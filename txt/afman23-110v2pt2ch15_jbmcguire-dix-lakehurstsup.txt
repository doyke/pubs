AFMAN23-110V2PT2CH15, Asset Reporting, Redistribution, And Disposal, dated 1 April
2009 is supplemented as follows: This publication provides instructions and procedures
required to carry out the Air Force policy and implementation of local Air Force Manual 23-110,
USAF Supply Manual, policy as it applies to all Joint Base Mcguire-Dix-Lakehurst (JB MDL)
supply users/customers. This publication applies to all host and tenant units assigned or attached
to Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst, to include Air National Guard (ANG) and Air Force
Reserve Command (AFRC). This publication implements AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2,
Chapter 15, Asset Reporting, Redistribution, and Disposal. It applies to individuals at all levels
who prepare, manage, review, certify, approve, disseminate and/or use official Air Force
publications and forums, including Air National Guard units and Air Force Reserve Command.
Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained In Accordance With (IAW) Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of IAW with the Air Force Records Information Management System
(AFRIMS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Refer recommended
changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR)
using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from
the field through Major Command (MAJCOM) publications/forms managers. This publication
may not be supplemented.
