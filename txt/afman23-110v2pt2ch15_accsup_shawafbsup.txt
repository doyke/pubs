SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This document has been substantially revised and must be completely revised. Changes to
include inspection waiver, L and P equipment excess options.

Attachment 15B-10, paragraph 15B10.4.5. Delivery schedule to the Cargo Movement Element,
20 LRS/LGRDDC, will be as follows:

Table 15B10.1 (Added) Delivery Schedule. Table 15B10.1. (Added) Delivery Schedule.

 General Cargo                      02-13               0830-1430               0830-1430

Note: Any classified and Mission Capable (MICAP) shipments destined for stateside locations
delivered to Cargo Movement on Fridays will require 735 Supply Chain Management Group
(SCMG) furnished point of Contact (POC) and phone numbers at the destination for Saturday
delivery. This information will be verified by the Aircraft Parts Store (APS) or Traffic
Management Office prior to shipping. Classified shipments destined for overseas locations
cannot be accepted on Fridays due to Air Mobility Command (AMC) ports not accepting
classified shipments on weekends.

Attachment 15B-10, paragraph 15B10.6.2. The 20th Logistics Readiness Squadron (LRS)
Commander has opted to retain L and P equipment excess without reporting it to the Air Force
Equipment Management System (AFEMS) equipment excess program.

Attachment 15C-8, paragraph 15C8.3.3. The 20th Logistics Readiness Squadron (LRS)
Commander has opted for Storage and Issue to be responsible for validating the condition and
documentation of serviceable shipments with no additional signatures required with the
exception of classified, sensitive and Nuclear Weapons Related Material (NWRM).

Attachment 15C-22, paragraph 15C22.5.2. When the Standard Asset Tracking System (SATS)
is inline, printed copies of the DD Form 1348-1A, Issue Release/Receipt Document, for
shipments that are not required for use as a hand receipt for directed and non-directed shipments
of classified/sensitive items. When SATS or the Standard Base Supply System (SBSS) is not
offline and degraded operations is in effect, printed copies of the DD Form 1348-1A will be
printed and maintained for these assets.

Attachment 15C-28, paragraph 15C28.2. 735 SCMG Stock Control will be the primary POC
and handle all exception code management for all centralized and contingency bases. All initial
requests to load, change, or delete locally assigned shipment exception (SEX) code phrase
records will be submitted in writing through Compliance & Analysis, 20 LRS/LGLOC, and
signed by the Materiel Management Flight Chief prior to submission to the 735 SCMG. Letters
will include justification or reason, except notice code, exception phrase monitor and whether
Exception Control Code (ECC) documents are required. Approved requests will be submitted to
the 735 SCMG requesting that Exception Phrase Record (FXR) to load, change, or delete support

Attachment 15E-1, paragraph 15E1.2.8.1. Prior to turn-in (TIN) being processed, the generator
will initiate a written request to the Radiation Safety Office for subsequent inventory manager
disposal instructions as outlined in AFI 40-201, Chap 3.

Attachment 15E-1, paragraph 15E1.2.15. (Added) A degraded operations offline shipment
document number will be assigned from the offline control register maintained IAW AFMAN
23-110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chap 9/ACC Sup 1, Attachment 9B5.1, paragraph 9B5.1.1, as
supplemented.

Attachment 15E-12, paragraph 15E12.3.3. The 20th Logistics Readiness Squadron Commander
has opted for Storage and Issue to be responsible for validating the condition and documentation
of serviceable shipments with no additional signatures required with the exception of classified,
sensitive and Nuclear Weapons Related Material (NWRM).

Attachment 15E-13, paragraph 15E13.5. Inspection Section Responsibilities. The inspector
assigned to the applicable storage or TIN point (the applicable process owner) will perform the
responsibilities in this location.

Attachment 15E-13, paragraph 15E13.6 Pickup and Delivery Responsibilities. Vehicle
Operations, 20 LRS/LGRDDO, will assume all duties previously handled by Pickup and
Delivery in paras 15E1.2.11.1, 15E13.5.1.3 and 15E13.7, this manual.
