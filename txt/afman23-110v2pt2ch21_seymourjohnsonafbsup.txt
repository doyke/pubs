AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chap 21 is supplemented as follows. This supplement
implements procedures and guidance in AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chap 21, Special
Logistics Support Procedures. This supplement establishes additional requirements not
otherwise specified in this publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN)
33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Contact supporting records managers as
required. Send comments and suggested improvements to this supplement on AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, or via official correspondence with flight
commander concurrence to 4 LRS/LGLO, 1280 Humphreys St. Seymour Johnson AFB NC
27531.

       21.146.6.1. (Added) Monitor hazardous waste generated by LRS.
           21.146.6.1.1. (Added) When an item is identified as waste, HAZMART
           inspectors will process an FCC to condition H, and downgrade A5J to waste.
           21.146.6.1.2. (Added) The resulting A5J will be annotated IAW AFMAN 23-
           110, Vol 2, Part 2, Chap 21.
       21.146.6.2. (Added) Provide guidance and assistance when leaking or damaged
       container is discovered by LRS personnel.
           21.146.6.2.1. (Added) Submit letter to CEV requesting they provide required
           disposal information.
           21.146.6.2.2. (Added) Provide guidance and assist responsible section in
           preparing/packing waste items.
    21.146.7. (Added) Hazardous Materiel Handling Responsibilities:
       21.146.7.1. (Added) Place damaged/leaking containers in safe area to contain spill.
       21.146.7.2. (Added) Obtain any containers from CEV required for packaging of
       LRS generated hazardous waste.
       21.146.7.3. (Added) Ensure items are packaged as required by CEV or physically
       dispose of waste at approved accumulation points.
       21.146.7.4. (Added) Take waste to CEV for their certification and any additional
       paperwork required with a properly prepared A5J document prior to delivery to
       Defense Reutilization and Marketing Service (DRMS).
       21.146.7.5. (Added) Determine action when damaged or leaking containers are
       discovered while delivering property.
    21.146.8. (Added) All other LRS sections responsibilities:
       21.146.8.1. (Added) Place damaged/leaking containers in safe area to contain spill.
