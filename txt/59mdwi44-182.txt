This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive 44-1, Medical Operations. This
instruction describes the proper completion and submission of Wilford Hall Medical Center
(WHMC) Form 901, Film Printing Request. This instruction applies to all personnel assigned,
attached, or on contract to the 59 Medical Wing (MDW). This instruction does not apply to the
Air National Guard or Air Force Reserve. Refer recommended changes and questions about this
publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for
Change of Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this
publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management
System            Records            Disposition           Schedule           located           at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm

1. Overview. The purpose of WHMC Form 901, Film Printing Request, is to allow patients,
legal representatives, or providers to request printed copies of images of their Diagnostic
Imaging exams performed at Wilford Hall. It also allows patients to request compact discs
(CD)s to be burned with images of exams.
2. Personnel Responsibilities. File Room attendants will complete the WHMC Form 901 with
the patient’s, legal representative’s, or provider’s assistance. The information will be used to
access the patient’s diagnostic imaging “file” to print images or burn CDs. The form will be
maintained in the file room for one month and then will be purged.
3. Forms Submission. WHMC Form 901, Film Printing Request., is maintained in the
Diagnostic Imaging file room.
