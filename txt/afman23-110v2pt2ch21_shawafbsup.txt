AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 21, 1 April 2009, incorporating through Interim
Change, 1 April, 2011, is supplemented as follows: This publication applies to all personnel
assigned/attached to Shaw AFB, South Carolina. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual
(AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force
Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS)
located at https://my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Contact supporting records
managers as required. Refer recommended changes and questions about this publication to the
Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Form 847s to 20 LRS/LGLOC, 321 Cullen St., Shaw AFB, SC 29152-
5125; route through your appropriate functional chain of command.

21.99.5. The property and issue document will be forwarded to Vehicle Operations Cargo
Movement Team, 20 LRS/LGRDDO, who will deliver both the property and issue document to
the address given in the delivery destination block of the issue document. If the delivery
destination is missing/incorrect, the customer will provide the correct address to Customer
Support, 20 LRS/LGRMCC, to update the delivery destination record.
21.151.1. All hazardous waste turn-ins will be referred to the Central Accumulation Point
(CAP), 20 CES/CEVV, 895-0120. Each respective Logistics Readiness Squadron (LRS) waste
generating activity will assume responsibility to initiate waste disposal actions by contacting the
CAP.
21.162.5. The Equipment Accountability Element, 20 LRS/LGRMCE, is the single point of
contact (POC) for referral of inquiries regarding small arms serial number reports and data.
21.169.2. The Equipment Accountability Element, 20 LRS/LGRMCE, is the single POC for
referral of inquiries regarding Communication Security (COMSEC) serial number reports and
data bank interfaces and is responsible for researching the Consolidated Transaction History
(CTH), monthly confirmation or correction of all equipment (NF2/ND2) records errors, and
scheduling the semiannual reconciliations (R46). The Materiel Management Flight, 20
LRS/LGRM is responsible for researching the CTH and monthly confirmation and correction of
all non-equipment COMSEC (XB3.XD/XF) record errors identified on the R46.
