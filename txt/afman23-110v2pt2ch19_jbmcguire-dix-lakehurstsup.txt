AFMAN23-110 Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 19, 1 April 2009, is supplemented as follows:
This publication provides instructions and procedures required to carry out the Air Force policy
and implementation of local Air Force Manual 23-110 policy as it applies to all Joint Base
Mcguire-Dix-Lakehurst (JB MDL) supply users/customers. This publication applies to all host
and tenant units assigned or attached to Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst, to include Air
National Guard (ANG) and Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC). This publication implements
AFMAN 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 19, Stockage Policy. It applies to individuals at all
levels who prepare, manage, review, certify, approve, disseminate and/or use official Air Force
publications and forums, including ANG units and AFRC.

Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained In Accordance With (IAW) Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of IAW with the Air Force Records Information Management System
(AFRIMS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Refer recommended
changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR)
using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from
the field through Major Command (MAJCOM) publications/forms managers. This publication
may not be supplemented.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Recent changes to this document are substantially revised and must be completely reviewed.
19.5.1.4. All transfers to the Defense Logistics Agency Disposition Services for non-National
Stock Number (NSN) items will be referred to local Defense Logistics Agency Disposition
Services (DLADS), located at Fort Dix Joint Base consolidation point (Bldg 3137, ext. 562-
2509). Coordination of appointments will be accomplished prior to drop-off. All assets will
have six copies of DD Form 1348-1A, Issue Release/Receipt Document attached illustrating:
nomenclature, national stock number, unit of issue, quantity, and document number. Document
numbers will be assigned by 87 Logistics Readiness Squadron/Receiving Element at extension
754-4229 or 754-0128 for each DD Form 1348-1A.
19B33.4.2. Daily Document Register (D04) will be filed in the Mission Change Document file
