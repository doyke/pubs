This instruction is established to ensure that all affected staff agencies take the opportunity to review
OPLANS at least annually and make all necessary changes.

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS
This instruction has changes to the text in para. 1.1.2, a change in the individuals title in para 1.2.1. and
the signature block, and the references in para 2. A (|) indicates new or revised information since the last
version.



1. Procedures:
   1.1. The Directorate of Plans (XP) will:
       1.1.1. Maintain a status of plans computer printout to include the required review date as speci-
       fied in the plan.
       1.1.2. Forward the Review of Plans letter (attachment 1), or use AF Form 1768, Staff Summary
       Sheet, when changes to the plan are needed.
   1.2. Key staff agencies will:
       1.2.1. Review plans with their staff when requested by the Chief of Wing Plans.
       1.2.2. Forward recommended changes to plans under review to XP.
