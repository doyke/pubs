This supplement implements AFPD 36-28, Awards and Decorations Programs, and AFI 36-
2855, Judge Advocate General Awards, and describes The Judge Advocate General (TJAG)
awards presented to individuals in recognition of their outstanding service or contributions to
The Judge Advocate General’s Corps. It describes the annual and extraordinary achievement
awards and establishes nomination procedures. This supplement applies to Air Force Reserve
members in PACAF/JA offices. It does not apply to Air National Guard units. Refer
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility (OPR) using Air Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication; route AF Forms 847 from the field through the appropriate functional’s chain of
command. Submit requests for waivers through the chain of command to this Publication OPR
for non-tiered compliance items. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN)
33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Major changes include: corrects subparagraph numbers within paragraph 1, to track numbering
in AFI 36-2855; deletes PACAF-level award for Outstanding Paralegal Airman (AB-SrA) due to
new AF-level award for those grades.

1.1.10. (Added) PACAF Base Legal Office (Mahalo) of the Year Award. This award
recognizes outstanding achievement by a base legal office in leadership, achievements, and
community service. It is presented annually for the preceding calendar year.
2.1.1.1. (Added) NAFs will establish their own procedures to select nominees. Each NAF may
submit one nomination per award category, except for the Reichart Award. There is no limit on
the number of Reichart nominations that may be submitted. All Reichart Award nominations
satisfying the award criteria shall be forwarded to HQ PACAF for submission to AF/JAX.
3.1.1. (Added) The suspense for all packages is due to PACAF/JA on 1 March annually, or if 1
March falls on a weekend, the first duty day thereafter.
3.3.1.4. (Added) NAFs will submit packages, including Nomination Form, Biographical
Sketch, officer performance reports/civilian performance appraisals (Reichart Award only) and
official photograph, via electronic mail to PACAF/JA at pacaf.jav3@us.af.mil.
3.3.1.5. (Added) The PACAF Base Legal Office (Mahalo) of the Year Award is submitted in
bullet format on an AF 1206, Nomination for Award. The PACAF Base Legal Office (Mahalo)
of the Year Award is limited to one page in length (front side only).
5.4. (Added) Each PACAF-level winner will receive a plaque, and their photograph will be
displayed at HQ PACAF/JA.
