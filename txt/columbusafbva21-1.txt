                                WING FOD MONITOR




                                                                                                             TSgt Jeremy Lewis



 Involved in all Foreign Object Damage (FOD) issues

 Informs wing agencies of FOD hazards

 Periodically inspects and reports damaged pavement, flight line
  construction, and other hazards in or near aircraft parking ramps or
  taxiways to the airfield manager

 Monitors the status to ensure timely repairs

 Monitors FOD awareness and responsibilities briefing given to all
  flight line personnel
