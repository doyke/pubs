(Added) AFI 34-276, 1 November 1999, is supplemented as follows. This supplement implements and
extends the guidance of Air Force Instruction (AFI) 34-276, Family Child Care Programs dated, 1
November 1999. This instruction gives the requirements for operating family child care programs on
Vandenberg Air Force Base. It applies to licensing individuals living on base to be family child care
providers and for affiliating individuals living off base with the Air Force family child care program. It
authorizes the use of 30 SW Form 100, Unlicensed Family Day Care Report, and 30 SW Form 99,
Instructional Fire Evacuation Plan. This applies to all individuals caring for children of other families a
total of more than 10 hours a week on a regular basis and must be licensed to provide care in on-base
quarters. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with
the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.
