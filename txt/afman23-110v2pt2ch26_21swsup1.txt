The OPR for this supplement is TECOM/LGS (Mr. Paul L. Bentley). This supplement implements and
extends the guidance of Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, Volume 2, Part 2, Chapter 26, War
Readiness Materiel. This supplement describes 21SW procedures for use in conjunction with the basic
AFMAN. It applies to all Peterson AFB Standard Base Supply System (SBSS) functions. It also applies
to all activities assigned to and those activities supported by the 21st Space Wing. This supplement
applies to the US Air Force Reserve and Air National Guard Units.

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS

Requires War Readiness to review dated items in paragraph 26.8.2. Directs the use of CPS for manual
accounting in paragraph 26.26.2.2. Deletes paragraph 26.42.1. A bar ( | ) indicates a revision from the
previous edition.

26.8.2. War Readiness reviews dated items.

26.9.3. Store items requiring functional check in the normal mobility readiness spares package (MRSP)
locations.

26.26.2.2. Use the CPS procedures.
