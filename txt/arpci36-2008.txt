This instruction implements AFI 36-2005, Appointment in Commissioned Grades and
Designation and Assignments in Professional Categories â€“Reserve of the Air Force and United
States Air Force. This instruction outlines the preparation and use of ARPC IMT 70,
Computation for Grade Determination.


1. Purpose. This IMT is used to compute Grade Determination on each appointment package
application processed through ARPC/DPAR (accessions branch) in accordance with AFI 36-
2005.
2. Preparation. All blocks on this IMT are to be completed as required per each appointment
package application.
3. Procedures. This IMT is to be used by DPAR to determine Grade and to be routed with the
appointment package application to DPBPE (promotions) for Date of Rank.
