This instruction outlines the preparation and use of ARPC Form 83, Application for Retired Pay.
This instruction includes a requirement to maintain, collect, use, or disseminate information subject to the
Privacy Act of 1974. AUTHORITY: Title 10 U.S.C., Section 275.


1. Reference. Title 10 U.S.C., Section 1401, Computation of Retired Pay; AFI 36-3209, Separation and
Retirement Procedures for Air National Guard and Air Force Reserve Members; and E.O. 9397.

2. Purpose. ARPC Form 83 designates a Reserve memberâ€™s request to receive retired pay at age 60.

3. Preparation. The individual applying for Reserve retired pay at age 60 completes the entire form.

4. Procedures. ARPC Form 83 is sent to individuals, who qualify for Reserve retired pay at age 60,
approximately 4 months prior to their 60th birthday. The form is completed and sent back to ARPC, autho-
rizing ARPC to establish a retired pay account.

5. Form Prescribed. ARPC Form 83.
