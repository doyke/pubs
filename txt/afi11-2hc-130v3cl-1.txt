    This checklist establishes procedures for the operation of HC/MC-
    130 aircraft employed by Combat Air Forces (CAF). It applies to all
    HC-130 and Combat Search and Rescue (CSAR) MC-130P active
    duty, AFRC and ANG units and their assigned Back-up Aircraft
    Inventory (BAI) aircraft. Waivers are IAW with AFI 11-2HC-
    130V3. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed
    in this publication are maintained IAW
