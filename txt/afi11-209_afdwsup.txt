This supplement establishes policies, procedures and extends the guidance of Air Force
Instruction (AFI) 11-209, Aerial Event Policy and Procedures, 4 May 2006. This publication
does not apply to Air Force Reserve or Air National Guard units. Send AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, for this publication to AFDW/A3OV (Helicopter
Operations Section) for coordination. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management
of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information Management
System       (ARFIMS)        Records      Disposition    Schedule    (RDS)      located     at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm/.

1.1.1. (Added) General Guidance. This guidance does not apply to demonstrations as part of
training for specialized groups which may be involved in operational missions. These groups
include, but are not limited to, state/county search and rescue teams and local fire departments.
Demonstrations for these specialized groups will be coordinated at the unit level and do not
require a DD Form 2535, Request for Military Aerial Support. Participation approval is the
OG/CC.
1.4.4.1. (Added) Only AFDW aircrews certified according to AFI 11-246, Volume 6 will
perform aerial demonstrations.
1.6.3.1.2. Comply with altitude restrictions defined in AFI 11-2UH-1NV3 and applicable
supplements.
 2                                                AFI11-209_AFDWSUP 7 Novermber 2011


1.6.7. Waiver authority is AFDW/A3/5/9. Forward information to AFDW/A3OV to arrive at
HQ AFDW not later than 30 days prior to scheduled event.
2.4.3. Units will use the Request to Participate in Aerial Event Letter (Attachment #5) to
coordinate and obtain participation approval. The Request to Participate in Aerial Event Letter
will be forwarded though command channels to arrive at AFDW/A3 not later than 14 days prior
to the event.
2.4.12.1. (Added) Send any unique waivers, exceptions to policy, or requests not specifically
covered in this publication to AFDW/A3OV for evaluation and processing at the appropriate
level.
4.1. WG/CC is the approval authority for 1 HS helicopter static displays on Joint Base Andrews.
Static displays at public and military events elsewhere will be requested and approved using the
Request to Participate in Aerial Event Letter (Attachment #5).
4.4.6. (Added) Only AFDW aircrews certified according to AFI 11-246, Volume 6 will perform
aerial demonstrations.
5.11. Adopted Forms: Request to Participate in Aerial Event Letter – Attachment 5
