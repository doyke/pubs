This publication implements and extends the guidance of AFI 33-322. This supplement
establishes policies and procedures for the use of local Randolph AFB Forms by Enterprise
Information Management personnel. These forms are designed to provide Commanders,
Directors, and Civilian Leads a health assessment of their Records Management and Electronic
Records Management Programs. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the AF Records Disposition Schedule (AF RDS) located at:
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61/afrims/afrims.

6.1.11.1. (Added-Randolph AFB) To provide commanders, directors, and civilian leads a health
assessment of their unit Records Management (RM) and Electronic Records Management
(ERM) Programs, 12th Communications Squadron Enterprise Information Management (EIM)
personnel will utilize the following Randolph AFB Forms: Randolph AFB Form 45, Unit
Records Management (RM)/Electronic Records Managment (ERM) Health Assessment and
Randolph AFB Form 46, Staff Assistace Visit (SAV) - Trends and Analysis Report.

12.3. Forms Prescribed. Randolph AFB Form 45, Unit Records Management (RM)/Electronic
Records Management (ERM) Health Assessment and Randolph AFB Form 46, Staff Assistace
Visit (SAV) - Trends and Analysis Report.
