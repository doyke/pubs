JFTR, Volume 1, March 2007, is supplemented as follows: This supplement implements and
extends the guidance of JFTR, Volume I, Temporary Housing Allowance, and assigns
responsibility for support, control, and administration of Temporary Lodging Allowance (TLA)
for military service members in Alaska. Refer recommended changes and questions about this
publication to the office of primary responsibility (OPR) (see Attachment 1) using the AF Form
847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. Route the AF Form 847 through the
appropriate chain of command. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 37-123, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://afrims.amc.af.mil. This publication does not apply to the US Air Force Reserve or Air
National Guard units and members.

U9150 B.2. (Added) (11AF). In Alaska, the senior commander is the Eleventh Air Force
Commander. In accordance with the Joint Federal Travel Regulation, the incumbent must issue
written guidance for all services in the area. The Eleventh Air Force Commander delegates
authority to the installation commanders to judiciously administer TLA in accordance with the
JFTR.
