This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 65-1, Management of Financial
Services. It establishes procedures to assist 944th Fighter Wing (944 FW) personnel in
submitting financial documents or travel documents according to Department of Defense (DOD)
7000.14-R, Financial Management Regulations (FMRS), Volume 9, Chapter 8. This instruction
applies to all personnel assigned to the 944 FW. Refer recommended changes and questions
about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the Air Force (AF)
Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Forms 847 through unit
publication/forms managers. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in
this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information
Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS). This instruction requires
collecting and maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 5
U.S.C. 552.       System of records notice F065 AF SVA A, Non-Appropriated Fund
Instrumentalities (NAFI) Financial System.

1. General. This instruction covers general guidance for the preparation and submission of pay
and travel documents.
2. Documentation/Forms:
     2.1. 10 AF Form 11, Orders Request, or ARCNET will be used by the member to request
     orders to perform duty.
     2.2. AF Form 938, Request and Authorization for Active Duty Training/Active Tour, is used
     to order military personnel to report for military duty, away from or at home station. The AF
     Form 938 is also used to claim travel expenses (mileage only) to home station from
     member’s home of record (HOR) for personnel who live outside the corporate limits.
     2.3. DD Form 1610, Request and Authorization for Temporary Duty (TDY) Travel of DoD
     Personnel, is used to order civilian, Air Reserve Technician (ART) and Active
     Guard/Reserve (AGR) personnel to report for duty away from home station. DD Form 1610
     is used for RPA travel for those members on continuous RPA orders.
     2.4. DD Form 1351-2, Travel Voucher or Subvoucher, is used by all personnel traveling
     away from home station for travel expense reimbursement. DTS will be used for eligible
     orders.
     2.5. Air Education and Training Command (AETC) Form 180, Statement in Absence of
     Receipt, is used by the member when miscellaneous receipts have been lost.
3. Requirements:
     3.1. The Government Travel Card (GTC) will be used for payment of authorized
     government travel expenses. Airline ticket and fee; rental car, if applicable; lodging and all
     travel-related expenses will be listed on DD Form 1351-2. Note: All expenses of $75 and
     higher require an original receipt or a lost receipt using AETC Form 180.
     3.2. Split disbursement is required on all travel vouchers when the GTC has been used.
     3.3. Member will turn in pay documents to their unit prior to going home at the end of the
     tour, or utilize the AROWS-R Tour of Duty Certification (TODC) Program for eligible
     orders.Travel documents must be signed by the member, and signed/certified by a unit
     representative, and sent to 944 FW Voucher Examiner (944 FW/FMAT) in the Finance office
     within five (5) working days after completion of the tour. DTS will be used for eligible
     orders.
        3.3.1. One copy of the certified AF Form 938/DD Form 1610 with amendments, if
        applicable, and DD Form 1351-2 along with all travel-related original receipts and
        applicable checklists must be submitted to the 944FW/FMAT for travel pay.
        3.3.2. One certified AF Form 938 will be submitted to 944 FW Pay Office (944
        FW/FMAP) for Military Pay entitlements. Note: For overseas tours, attach a copy of
        Contingency Exercise Deployment (CED) (TE-Series) orders along with the AF Form
        938 or DD Form 1610 to the travel voucher.
     3.4. To avoid delinquent GTC accounts, members performing long tours (45 days or more)
     will complete an Accrual TDY Voucher Worksheet every 30 days while away from home
     station and fax or mail it to their unit orderly room. The unit will review, sign and date the

4. Responsibilities:
   4.1. Traveler. The traveler is responsible for preparing DD Form 1351-2 (see Attachment 2
   for instructions) to claim reimbursement for official travel expenses away from home station,
   and AF Form 938 (see Attachment 3) to claim military pay entitlements and/or travel pay
   (mileage only) from home station to home of record, if applicable. The traveler is
   responsible for the truth and accuracy of the information on the documents.
   4.2. Unit. Each unit is required to establish an effective program to ensure their members
   file travel and pay documents in a timely manner upon completion of duty. Each unit must
   provide assistance to their members in completing travel and pay documents. Supervisors
   and unit-appointed Quality Control Monitors (QCM) will assist members with their travel
   voucher.      The 944 FW/FMAT can assist with the completion of the AF Form 938, as
   needed.
   4.3. Supervisors or unit QCM will:
       4.3.1. Review AF Form 938 (see Attachment 3 for instructions) to ensure Blocks 36
       through 44 are completed for traditional reservists and blocks 36 through 45 completed
       for ARTs before it is sent to 944 FW/FMAP (military pay).
       4.3.2. Review DD Form 1351-2 to ensure:
          4.3.2.1. Ensure split-disbursement is identified and an amount is entered for payment
          to the GTC; if not identified, contact the member for an explanation.
          4.3.2.2. Ensure amount claimed for reimbursement matches the attached receipts for
          airline tickets, rental car, lodging and any items $75.00 or more.
          4.3.2.3. Ensure that the amount received is annotated in Block 9 if the traveler
          received an advance payment. If no advance was taken, then “none” is indicated.
          4.3.2.4. Ensure one certified copy of the member’s travel order(s)/amendment(s) are
          attached to the travel voucher.
          4.3.2.5. Ensure member signed and dated the travel voucher and the supervisor’s
          block is signed by the supervisor or unit QCM before it is sent to the 944FW/FMAT
          for payment.
       4.3.3. Complete and attach the Financial Services Office (FSO) Reserve TDY Voucher
       Checklist (see Attachment 4) to each document sent to 944FW/FMAT for travel expenses
       reimbursement (DD Form 1351-2 or AF Form 938), ensuring a quality review has been
       completed and a point of contact is identified to answer any questions.
       4.3.4. Provide a copy of each FSO Reserve TDY Voucher Checklist to the unit Resource
       Advisor (RA) or Cost Center Manager (CCM) for tracking purposes.
   4.4. RA/CCMs. Unit RAs or CCMs are responsible for the accounting of funds in their
   organization and required to:
       4.4.1. Sign 10 AF Form 11, ensuring funds are available when members request orders
       to perform duty; keep a ledger to maintain unit funds/balances.

    4.4.2. The copy of the FSO Reserve TDY Voucher Checklist is used as a tool, notifying
    the RA that travel documents have been submitted for payment. The Commander’s
    Resource Integration System (CRIS) or a copy of a paid voucher, are used as tools
    notifying the RA that payment was made. The Personnel Budgeting and Analysis
    System (PBAS) report is used as a tool notifying the RA that pay entitlements were
    made.
       4.4.2.1. The copy of the FSO Reserve TDY Voucher Checklist must be kept until
       verification can be made that the member’s GTC account and certified orders have
       been paid. Note: Once paid, the FSO Reserve TDY Voucher Checklist may be
       destroyed or filed with the Outstanding Travel Voucher (GOO) and the Undelivered
       Orders Outstanding (UOO) reports.
    4.4.3. Contact the member or member’s supervisor no later than 10 days after tour ends
    when a copy of the FSO Reserve TDY Voucher Checklist has not been received showing
    travel/pay documents that have been submitted for payment, and/or when transactions
    have not cleared through CRIS, PBAS, etc.
    4.4.4. Review and submit information on monthly UOO and GOO reports:
       4.4.4.1. The UOO (pay & allowances) report (see Attachment 5 for instructions) is
       required per Headquarters (HQ) Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC) Inspection
       Management System (IMS), Finance Management (FM) Key Processes FM 23, 24,
       and 25: FM-PBAS, Item 05.
       4.4.4.2. The GOO report (see Attachment 6 for instructions) is required by the 56
       CPTS/FMFS on a monthly basis. RAs and or CCMs will receive this report from 944
       FW/FMB by the suspense date to allow data to be consolidated to meet 56
       CPTS/FMFS suspense.

                                          Attachment 2
       DD FORM 1351-2, TRAVEL VOUCHER OR SUBVOUCHER CHECKLIST

A2.1. When completing a travel voucher print legibly, attach one copy of all orders, a copy of
lodging receipt and all other receipts for expenses of $75.00 or more incurred while on orders.
A2.2. Block 1: Place a mark in the Split Disbursement block if you used your Government
Travel Card. (Split disbursement is mandatory when the GTC is used for payment). Enter
amount to be immediately applied to the bill.
A2.3. Blocks 2, 3, 4, 5, 6a, 6b, 6c, 6d, 6e, and 7: Self-explanatory.
A2.4. Block 8: List all order numbers from AF Forms 938, block 32; and DD Forms 1610,
block 22, as applicable.
A2.5. Block 9: List only advances made to you by a military installation. This includes any
payment made to the GTC in 30-day increments. Do not include automated teller machine
(ATM) withdrawals. If no advance payments were received, enter NONE.
A2.6. Block 11: Self-explanatory. (See AF Form 938, block 8 & 9 or DD Form 1610, block 5
for applicable information.)
A2.7. Blocks 12, 13, and 14: Not applicable (used only for permanent change of station (PCS)).
A2.8. Block 15a: Directly below the date, enter the year, then below the year enter the day and
month for each departure and arrival.
   A2.8.1. Block 15b: You do not need to show all stops, but you must show where you
   changed the type of transportation, i.e., car to plane, plane to car, and if you remained
   overnight.
   A2.8.2. Block 15c: See reverse side of travel voucher for code.
   A2.8.3. Block 15d: See reverse side of travel voucher for code.
   A2.8.4. Block 15e: Enter total cost of lodging for each overnight stay.
   A2.8.5. Block 15f. You may claim privately owned vehicle (POV) round trip mileage at the
   beginning and end of orders or if a family member or another person drops you off or picks
   you up in your car.
A2.9. Block 16: Mark Owner/Operator block even if a family member drives your car. If a
passenger in someone else’s car, mark Passenger block.
A2.10. Block 17: “X” the block applicable to your TDY.
A2.11. Block 18: List expenses, i.e. authorized rental car, Visa card ATM usage fee, official
calls, total cost of lodging, or baggage fees, etc. Note: If rental car was approved after orders
were published and/or official calls are claimed, orders approving official must sign in block 21a.
A2.12. Block 19: See reverse for explanation.
A2.13. Block 20a and 20b: Member will sign and date the travel voucher.
A2.14. Block 20c and 20d: Supervisor/QCM will sign and date the travel voucher.
A2.15. Block 21a and 21b: Signature only if supplemental voucher.

                                         Attachment 3
     CERTIFICATION OF AF FORM 938, REQUEST AND AUTHORIZATION FOR
      ACTIVE DUTY TRAINING/ACTIVE DUTY TOUR CHECKLIST (ORDERS)

A3.1. Block 36a: Enter location from which you departed, i.e., Home of Record (HOR), time,
day, and month.
A3.2. Block 36b: Enter location of duty station plus time, day, and month that you arrived and
the mode of travel: POV, government plane (GP), and/or Government Transportation Request
(GTR), i.e., civilian plane.
A3.3. Block 36c: Enter duty location that you are departing from, the time, day, and month.
A3.4. Block 36d: Enter location of your final destination for this order, i.e., HOR or remain in
place (RIP) when you convert to another order.
A3.5. Block 37: Read and circle applicable statements.
A3.6. Block 38 and 39: Sign and date.
A3.7. Block 40: Must reflect the actual start and finish dates of duty only. Do not include
travel days.
A3.8. Blocks 40, 41, 42, 43 and 44: To be completed by the certifying official.
A3.9. Blocks 45 and 47:      Must be signed by the member’s civil service timekeeper, if
applicable.

           INSTRUCTIONS TO OBTAIN AND PROCESS THE UOO REPORT

A5.1. UOO - This listing is used to identify members that have not turned in their certified order
for Military Pay entitlements.
   A5.1.1. In PBAS Web, select Main Folder. Select Reports. Select Active Duty Training
   (ADT) Report Listing. Select Unpaid (UOO-Accounting Stage).
   A5.1.2. Select “Applicable Work Center”. Use down arrow and highlight your units’ code.
   Next, click "on or before.” Use the calendar to choose one day preceding current date.
   A5.1.3. Under Sort Options, click on. Social Security Number (SSN).
A5.2. Instructions to process the report:
   A5.2.1. RAs and/or CCMs will use PBAS to extract UOO reports for military pay.
   A5.2.2. If the order has been submitted for pay, indicate the date the order was sent to 944
   FW/FMAP, Pay Office.
   A5.2.3. If duty was not performed, indicate amendment data (order # and date).
   A5.2.4. If the traveler is TDY, annotate an estimated return date from TDY.
   A5.2.5. If the order has not been submitted for pay, notify the member’s supervisor to
   contact the member and indicate the date member was contacted and estimated date for
   submission.

                                         Attachment 6
  INSTRUCTIONS FOR UNITS TO COMPLETE OPEN TRAVEL ORDER LISTING
                              (GOO)

A6.1. The GOO report is sent to the unit RA/CCM by 944 FW/FMB 2-3 days prior to Unit
Training Assembly (UTA). This report lists personnel who have completed duty which requires
submission of travel pay documents. The unit is required to review the following general
procedures or actions while providing proper response status of outstanding orders:
A6.2. If an order was prepared and the member performed duty, indicate the date the voucher
was filed, or any voucher information relating to claim. If a voucher was not filed, the traveler
must be contacted immediately to file the voucher; indicate the date the traveler was contacted
and an estimated date of completion for this action. All travel vouchers must be filed unit prior
to going home at the end of the tour, but not later than five business days after completion of
travel. Documents must be signed by the member, certified, and sent to 944 FW/FMAT within
five working days after completion of the tour.
A6.3. If an order was prepared but the duty wasn’t performed, an amendment must be issued to
cancel the order. Provide amendment data (order # and date) in report. If the traveler is TDY,
annotate an estimated return date from TDY on the report.
