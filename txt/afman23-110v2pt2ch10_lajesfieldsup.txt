AFMAN23-110V2PT2CH10 is supplemented as follows. This supplement implements and
extends the guidance Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 23-110, USAF Supply Manual, 1 April 2008.
This supplement establishes Lajes Field policy and procedures for physical asset management.
These procedures apply to personnel assigned to the Material Management, Management and
Systems, and Vehicle Management Flights. Refer recommended changes and questions about
this publication to the OPR using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication;
route AF Form 847s from the field to the Lajes Field publications/form manager. Ensure all
records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records and disposed of in accordance with
the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-
af61a/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This supplement has been updated to reflect the current policies, processes, and procedures used
in the Air Force retail supply system to promptly receive, manage, select, and move material to
and from stock at the base retail level. This document is substantially revised and must be
reviewed entirely.
