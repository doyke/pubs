Air Force Instruction (AFI) 91-202, Mishap Prevention Program, 01 August 1998, is supplemented
as follows:
This supplement provides procedures for the United States Air Force Academy (USAFA) Mishap Preven-
tion Program. It applies to all military and civilian personnel including Air Force Reserve (AFRC) or Air
National Guard (ANG) members assigned to the USAFA. Refer recommended changes and conflicts
between this and other publications to HQ USAFA/SE 8110 Security Drive Door 18, USAF Academy, Co
80840, on Air Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication. Ensure that all records
created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN
37-123, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://afrims.amc.af.mil.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This interim change removes the requirement for spot inspections are maintained for three years, para-
graph 1.6.16.4.3. USRs will maintain a list of buildings belonging to the unit, paragraph 1.6.16.4.7.
USRs will brief their commander quarterly on the status of the units’ safety program, paragraph 1.6.16.5.
Spot inspections to be maintained for two years, current and previous, paragraph 3.1.3.3. A bar ( | ) indi-
cates revisions from the previous edition.

(DRU), Dean of Faculty (DF), Department of Athletics (AD), Preparatory School (PL) safety programs.

1.6.16.4.4. (Added) A written and current Job Safety Training Outline.
1.6.16.4.5. (Added) AF Form 55, Employee Safety and Health Record or reference location.
1.6.16.4.6. (Added) Documentation of safety-related unit level meetings, commander’s call and other
