AFI 36-802, Pay Setting, 01 September 1998, is supplemented as follows. This supplement
describes the procedures used at Minot AFB when setting rates of pay for appropriated fund
employees. This applies to all civilian personnel serviced by the Minot AFB Civilian Personnel
Flight. Send comments and suggested improvements on AF Form 847, Recommendation for
Change of Publication, to 5 FSS/FSMC.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES
Paragraphs 1.2.6. and 1.2.8. are renumbered to match the revised AFI 36-802, and to clarify local
pay setting procedures.
