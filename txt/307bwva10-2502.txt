                 PROCEDURES FOR BLDG
                            When Warned or Directed to Take Immediate Shelter:
                            Ensure other facility occupants are informed to take shelter.
                            If in a modular/non-permanent building, and time permits, relocate to
                            a more suitable structure or permanent facility -- use good judgment!
                            Report to the designated Natural Disaster shelter location IAW the
                            307 BW or Unit Sheltering Plan -- follow established procedures.
                            If shelter location is unknown; get to the lowest floor level, innermost
                            room away from windows and exterior facility walls or doors.
       Natural Disaster
                            Remain sheltered until the “All Clear” announcement is given.
          (Tornado)
                            Report personnel status to UCC, CSS or 307 BW Command Post.
                            When Warned or Directed to Shelter-In-Place due to
                            accidental/intentional Hazardous Materials or CBRN Release:
                            Ensure other facility occupants are informed to take shelter.
                            Report to the designated Hazardous Materials (HazMat) Release SIP
                            location IAW the 307 BW or Unit Sheltering Plan -- follow established
                            procedures and/or Facility Manager or senior member instructions.
                            If SIP location is unknown; get to the highest floor level, innermost
                            room with the fewest windows and doors. Shutdown heating,
                            ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. If an SIP Kit is not
       HazMat/CBRN
                            on hand, use available materials to seal cracks around doors or
          Release           windows and block air supply vents to keep vapors/gases out.
                            Remain sheltered until the “All Clear” announcement is given.
                            Report personnel status to UCC, CSS or 307 BW Command Post.
