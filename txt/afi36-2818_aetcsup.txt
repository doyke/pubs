This supplement implements and extends the guidance of AFI 36-2818, The USAF Maintenance
Awards Program, 27 November 2007. This supplement applies to all AETC units; it does not apply to
AETC-gained Air Force Reserve Command units or Air National Guard units. This publication requires
the collecting and keeping of information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by Title 10,
United States Code, Section 8013. System of records notice F036 AFPC V, Awards and Decorations,
applies. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and
disposed of in accordance with Air Force Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/.
SUMMARY OF CHANGES
This publication has been substantially revised and must be completely reviewed. Major changes
include removing all tables outlining specific award criteria (Tables 3.1, 3.2, and 3.7) and updating the
entire document to reflect changes to Air Force organizational structure.
4.1.1.1. This award is restricted to individuals who are in a maintenance Air Force specialty code
(AFSC) (2AXXX, 2PXXX, 2RXXX, or 2WXXX) and assigned to a maintenance group (MXG), aircraft
maintenance squadron (AMXS), maintenance squadron (MXS), component maintenance squadron
(CMS), or equipment maintenance squadron (EMS). Individuals who are in maintenance AFSCs but are
assigned to units whose primary mission is other than sortie generation or operational maintenance
missions are ineligible. Specifically excluded from consideration are individuals assigned to training
organizations (wings, groups, squadrons, or detachments), the Inter-American Air Forces Academy
(IAAFA), studies and analysis squadrons (SAS), recruiting, and quality assurance evaluator (QAE) or
quality assurance representative (QAR) positions.
4.1.1.3. Units will submit packages electronically to HQ AETC/A4MMR no later than 15 June.
4.1.2.1. This award is restricted to individuals who are in a maintenance AFSC (2AXXX, 2PXXX,
2RXXX, or 2WXXX) and assigned to a MXG, AMXS, MXS, CMS, EMS, or civil service equivalent.
