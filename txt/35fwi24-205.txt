This instruction implements Air Force Policy Directive (AFPD) 24-2, Preparation and
Movement of Air Force Materiel, and guidance memorandum dated 26 Nov 08 issued by
PACAF/A4, AFMAN 23-110, USAF Supply Manual, and AFI 24-203, Preparation and
Movement of Air Force Cargo. This instruction applies to the 35th Logistics Readiness
Squadron and all supported shipping organizations. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual
(AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with Air Force
Records Information Management System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS)
located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/.”              Additionally, if the publication
generates a report(s), alert readers in a statement and cite all applicable Reports Control Numbers
in accordance with AFI 33-324. Refer recommended changes and questions about this
publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from the field through the
appropriate functional’s chain of command.”

1. RESPONSIBILITIES:
   1.1. The 35th Logistics Readiness Squadron Commander (35 LRS/CC) will ensure all
   shipping organizations provide appointment letters for personnel authorized to sign and issue
   DD Forms 1149.
   1.2. The 35th Logistics Readiness Squadron, Materiel Management Customer Service (35
   LRS/LGRMCC) will ensure DD Form 1149 shipment procedures are incorporated into base-

    level supply customer training, Block I (General Supply Indoctrination), Block IIB (Repair
    Cycle Management) and Block III (Equipment Management) courses. Complete blocks 3
    thru 7 of the DD Form 1149 checklist and initial.
    1.3. The Traffic Management Officer (TMO) (35 LRS/LGRD) will ensure organizations
    provide a completed/signed DD Form 1149 checklist prior to shipping all commodities via
    DD Form 1149.
       1.3.1. Provide guidance to organizations to establish an account to access the web-based
       DD Form 1149.
       1.3.2. Ensure a copy of all units’ appointment letters are maintained            to verify
       authorization to ship cargo.
       1.3.3. Ensure the completed DD Form 1149 are filed with applicable shipment
       documents.
    1.4. All shipping organizations will appoint in writing personnel authorized to sign and issue
    the DD Form 1149. Complete first two checklist items on the DD Form 1149 checklist,
    initial, print, sign and date checklist.
2. PROCEDURES:
    2.1. The organization shipping cargo will complete the DD Form 1149 online at
    https://www.afmc-mil.wpafb.af.mil/HQ-AFMC/LG/LSO/lol and in accordance with AFI
    24-203, Attachment 2 to include a complete description of items being shipped. Annotate
    assigned Transportation Control Number issued by web site on the top right corner of the DD
    Form 1149 checklist, complete the first two checklist items, initial, print, sign and date
    checklist. Organization will proceed to 35 LRS/LGRMCC to ensure item being shipped on
    DD Form 1149 is authorized to be shipped.
    2.2. 35 LRS/LGRM Customer Service will reconcile and validate information on DD Form
    1149 to ensure item is not accountable. Once cargo is validated, 35 LRS/LGRM Customer
    Service will complete checklist items 3 thru 7 of the DD Form 1149 checklist, initial, print,
    sign and date checklist. 35 LRS/LGRM Customer Service will direct member to Cargo
    Movement Section (35 LRS/LGRDDC) to ship cargo.
    2.3. 35 LRS/LGRDDC will verify appointment letter authorizing personnel to ship cargo.
       2.3.1. Validate DD Form 1149 checklist and complete checklist items 8 and 9 of the DD
       Form 1149 checklist, initial, print, sign and date checklist.
       2.3.2. Ship item to authorized location, and ensure the completed DD Form 1149
       shipment checklist are filed with applicable shipping documents.

3. ADOPTED FORMS:

DD Form 1149, Requisition and Invoice/Shipping Document
