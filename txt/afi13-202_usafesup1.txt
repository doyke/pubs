AFI 13-202, 18 March 1994, is supplemented as follows:
This supplement amplifies procedures in AFI 13-202, Overdue Aircraft. Units under the operational con-
trol of the Commander, US Air Forces in Europe (USAFE/CC) will comply with this supplement.

SUMMARY OF REVISIONS
This supplement is new and contains instructions specific to US Air Forces in Europe.
1.1. Wing commander or installation commander . The wing or installation commander will estab-
lish flight monitoring procedures for arriving and inbound aircraft.
1.1.2.2. Notify the USAFE Command Center, Ramstein Air Base, Germany, at DSN (314) 480-8200,
commercial 49-(0)6371-47-8200. (USAFE will stand up a “crisis Rescue Coordination Center” at Ram-
stein.)
1.3.1.2. De-alert all agencies if the location and safety of the OVERDUE aircraft is ascertained by the
EXCOM search.
