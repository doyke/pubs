This supplement implements and extends the guidance of DOD 4525.8_AFMAN 33-306, AF
Supplement, dated 12 Oct 06. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes prescribed
in this publication are maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363,
Management of Records, and are disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records
Information Management System (AFRIMS) AF Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/. Refer recommended changes and questions
about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847 from the field through the
Vance AFB Publications and Forms Manager. (AF Form 847 is prescribed in AFI 11-215,
USAF Flight Manuals Program [FMP]. Refer to that publication for guidance on filling out the
form.)

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Change all Base Information Transfer Center references to Official Mail Center (OMC).

C1.5.9.1. (Added)(VANCEAFB) Consolidated mail service is used for official mail sent to
Randolph AFB TX.

C1.5.9.2. (Added)(VANCEAFB) Forward mail for activities on Randolph AFB to contractor,
Base OMC in an appropriate container (envelope, box, Optional Form 65-B/C, U.S. Government
Messenger Envelope) addressed to the appropriate unit and functional address, i.e., HQ
AETC/SCMC. Except for Optional Forms 65-B/C, containers will show the sender's and
addressee’s complete mailing address.

C1.10.8.3.1. (Added)(VANCEAFB) Items requiring overnight service through Federal Express
must be hand carried to Freight Department, Bldg 200, by 1400. A letter of authorization must
accompany all items. All items requiring overnight services through the U.S. Postal Service
(USPS) must be hand carried to OMC (Official Mail), Bldg 430, by 1430, for postal pickup. If
overnight services are needed after the USPS pickup time, the OPR will hand carry the item to
the U.S. Post Office downtown. The USPS will not accept overnight packages after 1700. The
USPS does not guarantee overnight services to all areas.

C1.6.1a. Freight Department is the authorized shipping point for commercial small package
carriers.

Chapter DLI Definitions

DLI.1.64. Agencies authorized to deal directly with the Vance Branch (USPS) for mail receipt
and delivery are contractor Logistics and Army/Air Force Exchange Service.

                           CHAPTER 8 (Added) (VANCEAFB)

C8.8.1. (Added)(VANCEAFB) Receipt for accountable mail (registered, certified, and insured)
by signing a DD Form 2825, Internal Receipt, with black ink pen. Type, stamp, or print name
the way the person signs it. When accountable mail is delivered without the DD Form 2825, the
activity distribution office will return it immediately to contractor OMC for accountability
control.
