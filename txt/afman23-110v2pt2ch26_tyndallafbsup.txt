SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The summary of change includes who reviews dated items, who manages the Functional Check
Program, Permanent Mission Support Kit (PMSK) changes, and who is responsible for secure
storage, issue, and management of all mobility bags with the exception of A and B bags for
Security Police (325 SFS); and Civil Engineering (325 CES/CED).

26.8.2. Inspection Function will review dated items.
26.9.1. Inspection manages the Functional Check Program.
26.13. When a WRM kit or package is received on a transfer or loan, a complete inventory is
accomplished within 10 working days by Central Storage personnel.
26.26.2.2. The applicable listing and a log book will be used to manually account for all the
short-term deployment transactions.
26.31.1. A Permanent Mission Support Kit (PMSK) consisting of peacetime operating stock is
authorized to support recurring exercises and deployments. The PMSK is assembled only when
325 MSG/LGRR provides deployment notification. The deploying unit is responsible for
scheduling FKD inputs to deploy and returning the kit. The deploying unit will process PMSK
issues to initially fill the kit, additionally they will assemble manage and maintain accountability
of property in the PMSK. The deploying unit will process deployment issues for parts consumed
from the kit. Upon return the deploying unit will turn-in all unused serviceable PMSK quantities
into base supply stocks and route consumed due-in-from-maintenance (DIFM) parts into the
appropriate repair channels. The deploying unit will initiate Report of Survey for any lost
property.
26.45.1. The Individual Protective Equipment Function is responsible for secure storage, issue,
and management of all mobility bags with the exception of A and B bags for Security Police
(325 SFS); and Civil Engineering (325 CES/CED).
