This publication supplements requirements of AFI 32-6001, Family Housing Management, para 1.3.3
and 1.3.8 and applies to all personnel assigned or attached to Kirtland Air Force Base. The following
defines the prioritized categories of referral for housing on Kirtland Air Force Base. Personnel will be
referred for housing in priority from categories A through D. Send comments and suggestions about this
publication for improvements on AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication, to the
Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR). Ensure that all records created as a result of processes
prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of
Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located
at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61a/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

2. Category B: Duty requirements of the following positions mandate the incumbent reside on base.
Individuals filling these positions will be referred for the next available housing in their grade and
bedroom category. The 377th Air Base Wing Commander (377 ABW/CC) may waive this requirement
when adequate privatized housing is not available.

3. Category C: Organizational Commanders, at squadron level and above, with Uniform Code of
Military Justice (UCMJ) authority may better serve the government by living on base. If they choose,
individuals filling these positions will be referred for the next available house in their grade and
bedroom category after individuals in Categories A & B are referred. Official orders or assignment
announcement letters appointing individuals as commanders with UCMJ command authority is required
for Category “C” housing priority.
4. Category D: All other personnel eligible for housing will be referred to the housing developer IAW
AFI 32-6001, after personnel in Categories A, B, and C are referred.
