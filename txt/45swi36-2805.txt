1. The 45 SW/CC and 45 SW/CCC Recognition Coin:
   1.1. This instruction provides the 45 SW Commander and Command Chief Master Sergeant
   with the means to provide on-the-spot recognition and appreciation awards to individuals that
   have contributed to the 45 SW mission accomplishment and have merited immediate
   recognition.
 2                                                                                  45SWI36-2805


     1.2. The 45 SW Commander’s coin will be presented by the wing commander or designated
     representative to assigned military and civilian employees, who, by their dedicated individual
     effort, have facilitated the successful accomplishment of a particular program, function, task,
     or mission, as authorized in AFI 36-2805 AFSPC Sup 1 para 1.4.6. The 45 SW Command
     Chief Master Sergeant’s coin will be presented by the 45 SW Command Chief Master
     Sergeant.
     1.3. Coins may not be used as personal or commemorative gifts, souvenirs, tokens of
     exchange in games, or as morale-builders unrelated to special achievements.
        1.3.1. Coins may not be used as tokens of appreciation for government officials, foreign
        officials or non- Federal personnel in recognition of general support of improved
        community relations.
2. Group and Squadron Level Recognition and Appreciation Coins. Group and squadron
commanders are authorized one coin award program per each group and squadron. Units below
squadron level are not authorized a coin award program.
     2.1. Coins will be unit funded. All guidelines and limitations in 1.3 and 1.3.1 apply to
     group/squadron coin programs.
     2.2. Prior to implementing a coin award program, groups/squadrons must coordinate and
     approve a unit- level policy directive (instruction, policy letter, ect) that establishes and
     details their specific program.
     2.3. Coins purchased with personal or private funds do not fall under the limitations of this
     instruction.
