This Instruction implements 32-10, Installation and Facilities, and outlines responsibilities and
clarifies procedures for the Luke’s Place facility/scheduling managers and Luke’s Place users to
properly utilize the facility and the contents therein. It applies to all units assigned or attached to
Eglin Air Force Base, to include any associate organizations and off-base and remote site units
who use and service Luke’s Place. Send recommended changes or comments for this publication
to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the Air Force (AF) Form 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

1. Introduction. Luke’s Place is a premier military facility and is intended to be used for
official military functions.     This instruction sets procedures for the Luke’s Place
facility/scheduling managers and users. It describes their duties and the minimum requirements
for scheduling, managing, safeguarding and utilizing the facility.
2. Facility Management. 96 LRS/CCE, located in building 600, room 106, is the OPR for
managing the SharePoint scheduling site and is responsible for signing out the facility key, and
scheduling routine maintenance of Luke’s Place with CE Maintenance and Repair. The facility
is under custodial contract.
3. Scheduling Management. 96 LRS/CCE responsibilities do not include making phone
reservations. All personnel desiring to utilize Luke’s Place will use the SharePoint site to make
their reservation.

NOTE: 96 LRS reserves the right to cancel/reschedule other users’ function if the date/time
conflicts with a 96 LRS function.
4. User Responsibilities:
     4.1. Access 96 LRS/CCE SharePoint scheduling site to reserve Luke’s Place at:
     https://org.eis.afmc.af.mil/sites/96abw/96msg/96lrs/lukesplace/default.aspx
     4.2. Pick up the facility keys from 96 LRS/CCE and return to 96 LRS/CCE immediately
     after user’s event has concluded.
     4.3. Ensure Luke’s Place is neat and orderly.
     4.4. Provide your own audio/visual support or contact 46th Test Wing Presentations at 850-
     882-2109, DSN 872-2109 for assistance.
     4.5. Ensure all lights are turned off, exit doors are secure, tables and chairs are set back to its
     original configuration and main entrance door is locked before leaving. Also, if the facility is
     left unclean, the First Sergeant of the organization who used the facility last will be notified.
5. Incident Reporting. Without delay, during normal duty hours (Monday-Friday, 0700-1600),
contact 96 LRS/CCE at 850-882-4300 or 96lrs.cc@eglin.af.mil regarding emergencies or
facility repair or maintenance issues within Luke’s Place. 96 LRS/CCE will immediately notify
796 CES Customer Service at 882-2477/3177 to submit a work order or call in an emergency
work request.
