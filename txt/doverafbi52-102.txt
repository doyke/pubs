This instruction prescribes Dover Form 6, Catholic Community Religious Education Program
Student Enrollment Form, and Dover Form 7, Child and Youth Registration Form, and the
procedures used in completing these forms. This instruction requires collecting and maintaining
information subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, AFI 33-360, Publications and Forms
Management, AFI 33-322, Records Management Program, Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-
363, Management of Records, and AFI 33-332, The Air Force Privacy and Civil Liberties
Program. It applies to all personnel requesting to register their child and/or youth for chapel
programs and Catholic religious education, 436th Airlift Wing Chapel. Ensure that all records
created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained IAW AFMAN 33-
363, Management of Records, and disposed of IAW Air Force Records Information Management
System (AFRIMS) Records Disposition Schedule (RDS). Refer recommended changes and
questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the Air
Force (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847s from
the field through the appropriate functional’s chain of command.

1. PURPOSE: Dover Form 6 is used by the Chapel to register Catholic children and youth for
Religious Education. Dover Form 7 is used by the Chapel to register children and youth for
chapel events.
2. PROCEDURES: Dover Form 6 and 7 are made to be self-explanatory. If there are any
questions you can contact the Dover AFB Chapel, the Catholic Religious Education Coordinator,
the Protestant Religious Education Coordinator, or the event coordinator.
