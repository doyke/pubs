AFMAN 23-110, Volume 6, Chapter 4, is supplemented as follows: This supplement implements
procedures and guidance in AFMAN 23-110, Vol. 6, Chap 4, Precious Metal Recovery Program
(PMRP). This supplement establishes additional requirements not otherwise specified in this
publication. Ensure that all records created as a result of processes in this publication are maintained in
accordance with AFPD 37-1, Information Management, and AFMAN 37-123 (will convert to AFMAN
33-363), Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records
Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at https://webrims.amc.af.mil. Contact supporting records
managers as required. Send comments and suggested improvements to this supplement on IMT 847,
Recommendation for Change of Publication, or via official correspondence with flight commander
concurrence to 4 LRS/LGLOC, 1280 Humphreys Street, Seymour Johnson AFB, NC. 27531.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

The installation commander has delegated the authority to appoint a PMRP manager to the Mission
Support Group Commander. The PMRP manger will prepare a schedule and visit each participating
activity at least once every 24 months instead of once every 12 months. Items annotated with an asterisk
(*) denotes substantive content changes from previous edition.
4.2.1. The Mission Support Group Commander is authorized to appoint the installation PRMP manager.
The PMRP manager and assistant are assigned from the Materiel Management Flight, (LGRM) Supply
Chief Inspector. Also see AFMAN 23-110, Vol 2, Pt 13, Chapter 1, Standard Base Supply Procedures,
Para 1.15. SJAFB SUP1.
