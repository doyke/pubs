This mission directive prescribes the mission, organization, and responsibilities of the 10th Air
Base Wing, Headquarters United States Air Force (USAF) Academy, Colorado. Refer
recommended changes and questions about this publication to the Office of Primary
Responsibility (OPR) using Air Form (AF) Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are
maintained in accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records,
and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm.

SUMMARY OF CHANGES

Mission was corrected to properly capture emergency response functions.                Installation
Commander responsibilities were added.
1. Mission. To provide base-level emergency response functions, support functions, and
personnel in operation and maintenance of the USAF Academy.

3. Roles and Responsibilities. The Commander, 10th Air Base Wing:
     3.1. Provides base-level Mission Support functions for all DoD units and personnel assigned
     to the USAF Academy as well as dependents and retirees residing in the area. These include,
     but are not limited to: Command Post, Civil Engineer, Housing, Natural Resource
     Protection, Supply, Transportation, Contracting, Force Protection, Military and Civilian
     Personnel, Advanced Education, Family Support, Professional Military Education,
     Communications, Services, Military Equal Opportunity, and Civilian Equal Employment
     Opportunity functions.
     3.2. Provides base-level Health Care Support for all DoD units and eligible personnel
     assigned to the USAF Academy installation, as well as other eligible active duty, dependents,
     and retirees residing in the area. These include, but are not limited to: Primary, Routine and
     Acute Medical Care, Flight and Cadet Medicine, Occupational Medicine, Adolescent
