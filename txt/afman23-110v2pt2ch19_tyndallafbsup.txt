This publication supplements USAF Standard Base Supply System Stockage Policy. It applies
to all Air Force, contractor operated, Air Force Reserve, and Air Force Guard organizations
supported by Tyndall Base Supply unless otherwise noted in contract or support agreement terms
or conditions. Ensure all records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication
are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and disposed of in
accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition Schedule (RDS) located at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims/cfm. Send recommended changes and
questions about this publication to 325 MSG/LRD using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for
Change of Publication. This publication requires collecting and maintaining information
protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 9832 and E.O. 9397. System of
records notice F023 AF IL C applies.
