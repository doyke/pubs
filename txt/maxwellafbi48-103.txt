This instruction im plements Title 42, Public Health,        Clinical Laboratory Improvement
Amendments, and AFPD 48-1, Aerospace Medical Program. It prescribes the use of Maxwell
AFB IMT 365, Laboratory Test Requisition. This instruction requires collecting and
maintaining information protected by the Privacy Act of 1974 authorized by 10 U.S.C. 55, 10
U.S.C. 8013 and E.O. 9397. System of records notice FO44 AF SG E, Medical Record System,
applies. Maintain all records created as a resu lt of processes prescribed in this publication in
accordance with AFMAN 33-363, Management of Records, and dispose of them in accordance
with     the Air Force Records Dispos             ition   Schedule (RDS) (available at
https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61/afrims/afrims). This instruction applies to all personnel
authorized to request 42d Medical Group laboratory services.

1. Personnel needing laboratory services who do not have Computerized Health Care Services
(CHCS) access use MAFB IMT 365, Laboratory Test Requisition, to request services.

2. The provider indicates priority by checking th e appropriate block at the top of the IMT,
completes the patient identification inform ation, and indicates the required services by checking
the appropriate blocks. Annotate any com ments or special requirements in the space provided
on the reverse side. Providers must sign the request in order to receive services.

3. IMT prescribed: MAFB IMT 365, Laboratory Test Requisition.
