AFI 36-2302, 11 July 2001, is supplemented as follows:

This supplement implements AFI 36-2302. It establishes policies and procedures for
professional development in the Air University (AU) Professional Continuing Education (PCE)
program. Refer recommended changes and/or corrections to this publication to the Office of
Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF Form 847, Recommendation for Change of
Publication, through your chain of command. Ensure that all records created as a result of
processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in accordance with AFMAN 33-363,
Management of Records, and disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Disposition
Schedule (RDS) located at https://www.my.af.mil/gcss-af61/afrims/afrims. This supplement
applies to AU PCE schools.

2.3.9. (Added)(AU) PCE School Responsibilities:
2.3.9.1. (Added)(AU) All AU PCE schools or institutions hosting instructional programs
coordinate with Academic Affairs Division (HQ AU/CFA) during development or review of
internal regulations, operating instructions, unit-generated Instructional System Development
(ISD) modifications to the basic US Air Force ISD model, and manuals that affect the ISD
process as applied at AU.
2.3.9.2. (Added))AU) PCE schools develop local procedures to:

2.3.9.2.1. (Added)(AU) Provide orientation and training to instructors, faculty supervisors, and
administrators to ensure effective implementation and management of ISD in their school.
2.3.9.2.2. (Added)(AU) Ensure to the maximum extent possible the use of ISD for
development of instruction by each level of instructional developers, instructional managers, and
instructional supervisors within the organization.
2.3.9.3. (Added)(AU) Commanders and commandants of AU PCE schools identify
instructional program requirements based on sound analysis of the instructional situation as
described by the ISD process.
