      Summary: This change modifies the CFETP Part I and Part II: updating terminology; training
      references; and reassignment of proficiency codes from the 5-Skill Level and 7-Skill Level OJT
      columns to the 5-Skill Level and 7-Skill Level CDC columns. This change also includes updates
      to the 3-Skill Level proficiency codes to meet AFSC curriculum requirements. The guidance on
      structure, content design, and development of this CFETP is IAW AFI 36-2201, Air Force
      Training Program.
