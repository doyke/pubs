This supplement implements and extends the guidance of Department of Defense Instruction
(DoDI) 5330.03, Document Automation and Production Service (DAPS), 8 February 2006, and
Air Force Instruction (AFI) 33-395 Supplement, 28 February 2007. It prescribes procedures for
obtaining services from the Vance Air Force Base (AFB) Reprographics office and applies to all
Air Force military, civilian, and contractor personnel under contract by the Department of
Defense (DOD) who request services from the reprographics office. This publication also applies
to Air Force Reserve Command (AFRC) and Air National Guard (ANG) Units. Ensure that all
records created as a result of processes prescribed in this publication are maintained in
accordance with Air Force Manual (AFMAN) 33-363, Management of Records, and are
disposed of in accordance with the Air Force Records Information Management System
(AFRIMS)       Air     Force    Records    Disposition    Schedule     (RDS)     located     at
https://www.my.af.mil/afrims/afrims/afrims/rims.cfm. Refer recommended changes and
questions about this publication to the Office of Primary Responsibility (OPR) using the AF
from 847, Recommendation for Change of Publication; route AF Form 847 from the field
through the Vance AFB Publications and Forms Manager. (AF Form 847 is prescribed in AFI
11-215, USAF Flight Manuals Program [FMP]. Refer to that publication for guidance on filling
out the form.)

5.5.1.9.1. (Added) (VANCEAFB). Vance AFB Form 32, Printing and Collating Dummy
Master, will assist the Reprographic technician in ensuring the product is printed and collated
correctly. Customers are required to complete and submit it with Vance AFB Form 5604,
Reprographics Work Order, on any order that contains more than two originals.

5.5.1.10.1. (Added) (VANCEAFB). Vance AFB Form 5604 will be used to obtain all services
from the Reprographics office. The signature of the Government-Wide Purchase Card (GPC)
cardholder authorizing the printing must be obtained prior to the job being started. A signature
of the GPC cardholder is not required for any services being rendered other than printing. This
form will also be used in the billing process for all printing using DAPS ONLINE (DOL).
